## 1.1.目的
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">1.1. Purpose</span>

* 基于区块链技术以推动“公民建国运动”的主权区块链系统，创立公民链，采取去中心化民运，以建立自由民主的中华民族联邦共和国；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">CitizenChain is a sovereign blockchain system founded on blockchain technology to advance the Citizen Nation-Building Movement. It adopts a decentralized democratic movement model to establish a free and democratic Federal Republic of the China Nation.</span>
* 公民链是一条不受任何机构掌控的主权区块链，一个所有人都能自由使用的法定数字货币系统，一条所有中华民族联邦共和国公民都能参与投票的区块链；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">CitizenChain is a sovereign blockchain not controlled by any institution, a legal digital-currency system freely usable by everyone, and a blockchain on which all citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation may participate in voting.</span>
* 公民链依据《公民宪法》和《公民链白皮书》发行法定数字货币公民币，公民币是中华民族联邦共和国法定数字货币，是公民链治理货币；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">CitizenChain issues the legal digital currency Citizen Coin pursuant to the Citizen Constitution and the CitizenChain Whitepaper. Citizen Coin is the legal digital currency of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and the governance currency of CitizenChain.</span>

  <span class="whitepaper-en">Name: 公民链, English name: CitizenChain. Native digital currency: Citizen Coin. Symbol: GMB.</span>

  <span class="whitepaper-en">Citizen Declaration: humanity precedes the state. Citizens establish the state; the state belongs to citizens; citizens govern the state; the state does not rule over citizens; and citizens have no obligation of patriotism. The founding principle of state power is to protect citizens' rights to life, liberty, property, resistance against oppression, and to vote and stand for election from any unlawful infringement. When state power cannot guarantee this basic principle, citizens have both the right and the duty to overthrow that regime and establish a regime whose founding principle is the protection of citizens' rights to life, liberty, property, resistance against oppression, and to vote and stand for election.</span>

  <span class="whitepaper-en">State name and the Five Civic Principles: the state name Federal Republic of the China Nation is rooted in the long history and splendid culture of all nationalities of China. Its full name is the Federal Republic of the China Nation, and its official short name is China Federation. The Federal Republic of the China Nation is a federal republic committed to implementing the Five Civic Principles: citizen self-governance and citizen governance of the state (civic governance), democratic republicanism (democracy), protection of citizen rights (civil rights), building a society that safeguards citizens' livelihood (civic livelihood), and revival of the cultures of all nationalities (national cultures).</span>

## 2.2.国储会权威节点
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">2.2. National Citizen Reserve Committee Authority Node</span>

* 有1个国储会权威节点，不能增删改，拥有19个管理员，分别对应国家储备委员会的19个委员；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">There is one National Citizen Reserve Committee authority node. It cannot be added, deleted, or modified. It has 19 administrators, corresponding respectively to the 19 members of the National Citizen Reserve Committee.</span>
* 国储会拥有19票投票权和4个多签名治理账户（主账户+费用账户+安全基金账户+两和基金账户），采用N=19，T≥13签名，通过则19票同时生效，反之则同时否决；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The National Citizen Reserve Committee has 19 votes and four multisignature governance accounts: a main account, a fee account, a security fund account, and a Reconciliation Fund account. It uses N=19 and threshold T>=13 signatures. If approved, all 19 votes take effect simultaneously; otherwise, they are all rejected simultaneously.</span>
* 国储会拥有发起“发行数字公民币、销毁数字公民币、协议升级、更换国储会多签管理员、增删改新省储会/新省储行”等提案权，承担数据归档和节点引导；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The National Citizen Reserve Committee has the authority to initiate proposals including issuing digital Citizen Coins, destroying digital Citizen Coins, protocol upgrades, replacing National Citizen Reserve Committee multisig administrators, and adding, deleting, or modifying new Provincial Citizen Reserve Committees or Provincial Citizen Reserve Banks. It also undertakes data archiving and node bootstrapping.</span>
* 国储会和省储会负责区块的最终性确认，由一套最终性验证密钥负责投票，此投票仅属于区块的最终性确认投票，投票由国储会1票+43个省储会每省1票=44票，大于等于30票即生成确定性最终性；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The National Citizen Reserve Committee and Provincial Citizen Reserve Committees are responsible for block finality confirmation. A set of finality validation keys is responsible for voting, and this vote is only for block finality confirmation. The vote consists of one National Citizen Reserve Committee vote plus one vote from each of the 43 Provincial Citizen Reserve Committees, for a total of 44 votes. At least 30 votes generate deterministic finality.</span>
* 国储会费用账户用于接收10%的链上交易手续费，且只能往主账户转账；安全基金账户用于接收10%的链上交易手续费，安全基金用于赔付用户因区块链技术原因造成的损失。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The National Citizen Reserve Committee fee account receives 10% of on-chain transaction fees and may only transfer funds to the main account. The security fund account receives 10% of on-chain transaction fees, and the security fund is used to compensate users for losses caused by blockchain technology reasons.</span>

## 2.3.省储会权威节点
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">2.3. Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee Authority Nodes</span>

* 初始43个省，每省1个省储会权威节点，初始权威节点不能增删改，每个节点拥有9个管理员，分别对应省储备委员会的9个委员；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">There are initially 43 provinces, each with one Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee authority node. These initial authority nodes cannot be added, deleted, or modified. Each node has nine administrators, corresponding respectively to the nine members of the Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee.</span>
* 每个省储会拥有1票投票权和2个多签名治理账户（主账户+费用账户），采用N=9，T≥6签名，签名通过则生效，反之则否决；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Each Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee has one vote and two multisignature governance accounts: a main account and a fee account. It uses N=9 and threshold T>=6 signatures. If the signatures pass, the decision takes effect; otherwise, it is rejected.</span>
* 省储会拥有发起“销毁数字公民币、更换省储会多签管理员、提案发行”等提案权，承担数据归档、节点引导和区块最终性投票。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">A Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee has the authority to initiate proposals including destroying digital Citizen Coins, replacing Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee multisig administrators, and proposing issuance. It undertakes data archiving, node bootstrapping, and block finality voting.</span>

## 2.4.省储行权益节点
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">2.4. Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank Stake Nodes</span>

* 初始43个省，每省1个省储行权益节点，初始权益节点不能增删改，每个节点拥有9个管理员，分别对应省储行的9个董事会成员；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">There are initially 43 provinces, each with one Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank stake node. These initial stake nodes cannot be added, deleted, or modified. Each node has nine administrators, corresponding respectively to the nine board members of the Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank.</span>
* 每个省储行拥有1票投票权和2个多签名治理账户（主账户+费用账户），采用N=9，T≥6签名，签名通过则生效，反之则否决；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank has one vote and two multisignature governance accounts: a main account and a fee account. It uses N=9 and threshold T>=6 signatures. If the signatures pass, the decision takes effect; otherwise, it is rejected.</span>
* 省储行费用账户用于链下清算网络，接收用户存款、支付用户链下交易付款、收取支付交易费等；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank fee account is used for the off-chain clearing network, including receiving user deposits, paying users' off-chain transaction payments, and collecting payment transaction fees.</span>
* 每个省储行拥有1个永久质押账户，永久质押账户只能接收数字公民币，用于永久质押省储行创立发行的数字公民币；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank has one permanent staking account. The staking account may only receive digital Citizen Coins and is used to permanently stake the digital Citizen Coins issued at the founding of the Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank.</span>
* 省储行拥有发起“销毁数字公民币、更换链下交易验证密钥、更换省储行多签管理员”等提案权，承担数据归档和节点引导。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">A Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank has the authority to initiate proposals including destroying digital Citizen Coins, replacing off-chain transaction validation keys, and replacing Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank multisig administrators. It undertakes data archiving and node bootstrapping.</span>

## 2.5.全节点
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">2.5. Full Nodes</span>

* 全节点是数据归档和新块铸块节点，负责数据归档、去中心化通信和所有新区块的铸造，新区块特指除创世区块以外的所有区块，采用PoW共识机制获得铸块权；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Full nodes are data-archiving and new-block minting nodes. They are responsible for data archiving, decentralized communication, and the minting of all new blocks. New blocks refer to all blocks other than the genesis block. Full nodes obtain block-minting rights through the PoW consensus mechanism.</span>
* 全节点数量不限，部署运行citizenchain（中文：公民链）的即全节点，任何组织、任何人均可下载安装节点软件成为全节点，全节点分为归档全节点、普通全节点和通信全节点这3种模式，默认启动为归档全节点模式；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The number of full nodes is unlimited. Any deployment that runs citizenchain (Chinese: 公民链) is a full node. Any organization or individual may download and install the node software to become a full node. Full nodes are divided into three modes: archive full nodes, regular full nodes, and communication full nodes. The default startup mode is archive full node.</span>
* 全节点在SFID系统注册为私法人股份公司（含隶属非法人）的，并在链上完成“清算行”注册的，可加入清算网络成为清算行节点，成为清算行节点的全节点，可为用户提供扫码支付的清算服务，并获得全部清算手续费；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">A full node that is registered in the SFID system as a private legal-person joint-stock company, including subordinate unincorporated entities, and that has completed on-chain clearing-bank registration, may join the clearing network as a clearing-bank node. After becoming a clearing-bank node, the full node may provide QR-code payment clearing services to users and receive all clearing fees.</span>
* 清算网络是连接去中心化金融与传统金融的纽带，把传统金融的银行、第3方支付，甚至每个有能力部署全节点的企业，均纳入公民链，为用户提供快速的去中心化金融服务。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The clearing network links decentralized finance with traditional finance. It brings traditional banks, third-party payment providers, and even every enterprise capable of deploying a full node into CitizenChain, providing users with fast decentralized financial services.</span>
* 权威节点、权益节点和清算行节点必须是归档全节点，其他任意节点可自行选择节点模式，归档全节点保存完整的区块链数据；普通全节点适用于大部分用户，不用保存完整的区块链数据，使用剪裁后的链数据，同时保留了挖矿、出块的能力，以节约用户的磁盘空间；通信全节点适用于不参与网络共识，不参与挖矿，只需要使用去中心化通信的用户，使用通信全节点模式的用户，可以把安装在电脑上的区块链软件作为wuminapp的p2p通信中继，以实现完全去中心化的即时通信。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Authority nodes, stake nodes, and clearing-bank nodes must be archive full nodes. Any other node may choose its node mode independently. Archive full nodes store complete blockchain data. Regular full nodes are suitable for most users: they do not need to store complete blockchain data, use pruned chain data, and still retain mining and block-production capability, thereby saving disk space. ## 5.2.投票引擎
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">5.2. votingengine</span>

* 投票引擎由内部投票、联合投票和公民投票模块组成，每种投票的最长时限均为“30天”，超时没有通过的则否决，投票统一使用区块高度计数；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The voting engine consists of the internal vote, joint vote, and citizen vote modules. The maximum duration of each type of vote is 30 days. A vote that does not pass before timeout is rejected. Voting uniformly counts time by block height.</span>
* 内部投票仅在国储会、各省储会、各省储行和各多签账户内部进行投票，且内部投票仅适用于“内部事项”，内部投票通过则执行，超期或未通过则否决；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Internal voting occurs only inside the National Citizen Reserve Committee, each Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee, each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank, and each multisig account. Internal voting applies only to internal matters. If an internal vote passes, it is executed; if it times out or fails to pass, it is rejected.</span>
* 联合投票是指国储会+全体省储会+全体省储行之间的联合投票，除由内部投票的事项外，其他所有需要投票的事项均是联合投票事项，联合投票结果为全票通过的则直接执行，非全票通过的或超期的则进入联合公投流程；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Joint voting means voting among the National Citizen Reserve Committee, all Provincial Citizen Reserve Committees, and all Provincial Citizen Reserve Banks. Except for matters handled by internal voting, all other matters requiring a vote are joint-vote matters. If a joint vote passes unanimously, it is executed directly; if it is not unanimous or times out, it enters the joint referendum process.</span>
* 联合公投是指所有经过公民认证的轻节点，进入联合投票提案快照的均可参与的投票，超过50%的可投票轻节点同意的则通过，超期或未通过的则否决；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">A joint referendum is a vote in which all citizen-certified light nodes included in the joint-vote proposal snapshot may participate. If more than 50% of eligible voting light nodes approve, it passes; if it times out or does not pass, it is rejected.</span>
* 国储会节点有19票，省储会节点每省1票共43票，省储行节点每省1票共43票，轻节点通过绑定身份识别码的拥有1票投票权；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The National Citizen Reserve Committee node has 19 votes. Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee nodes have one vote per province, totaling 43 votes. Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank nodes have one vote per province, totaling 43 votes. A light node that binds an identity identification code has one vote.</span>
* 投票资格由身份识别码系统判定，区块链仅负责提案期内一次性计票与结果不可篡改。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Voting eligibility is determined by the identity identification code system. The blockchain is responsible only for one-time counting during the proposal period and for making the result tamper-proof.</span>

* 投票分类：
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Voting categories:</span>
  * 内部投票/internal_vote.rs ：在国储会、各省储会、各省储行内部进行投票，投票阈值详见：primitives/src/count_const.rs常量文件；
    <span class="whitepaper-en">Internal vote / internal_vote.rs: voting inside the National Citizen Reserve Committee, each Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee, and each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank. For voting thresholds, see the constants file primitives/src/count_const.rs.</span>
  * 联合投票/joint_vote.rs ：联合投票直接记录各机构（国储会+各省储会+各省储行）内部投票结果，按以下“联合投票流程图”执行；
    <span class="whitepaper-en">Joint vote / joint_vote.rs: joint voting directly records the internal voting results of each institution, including the National Citizen Reserve Committee, each Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee, and each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank, and executes according to the following joint-vote flowchart.</span>
  * 公民投票/citizen_vote.rs ：使用SFID绑定的账户地址可参与投票，未绑定的不能投票，公民投票主要用于公权机构选举的链上投票；
    <span class="whitepaper-en">Citizen vote / citizen_vote.rs: account addresses bound with an SFID may participate in voting; unbound addresses may not vote. Citizen voting is mainly used for on-chain voting in public-authority institution elections.</span>

* 联合投票流程图：
```
① 国储会/省储会任意管理员发起联合投票提案
② 内部投票阶段：国储会19票+43个省储会43票+43个省储行43票=105票
③ 判断结果：
   ├─ 全票通过 → 直接执行
   └─ 非全票/超期 → 进入联合公投阶段(全民公投)
         ├─ >50% → 执行
         ├─ ≤50% → 否决
         └─ 超期  → 否决
```

  <span class="whitepaper-en">Joint-vote flowchart:</span>
```
1. Any administrator of the National Citizen Reserve Committee or a Provincial Citizen Reserve Committee initiates a joint-vote proposal.
2. Internal voting stage: National Citizen Reserve Committee 19 votes + 43 Provincial Citizen Reserve Committees 43 votes + 43 Provincial Citizen Reserve Banks 43 votes = 105 votes.
3. Result determination:
   ├─ Unanimous approval → execute directly
   └─ Non-unanimous approval / timeout → enter the joint referendum stage (nationwide referendum)
         ├─ >50% → execute
         ├─ <=50% → reject
         └─ timeout → reject
```

## 5.5.交易模组
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">5.5. transaction</span>

### 5.5.1.链上交易
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">5.5.1. onchain-transaction</span>

* 链上交易手续费为0.1%，精度为四舍五入后的小数点后两位，且单笔支付手续费最少为0.1元，不足0.1元的以0.1元计算，由付款方必须支付，交易费按8:1:1的比例分账；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The on-chain transaction fee is 0.1%, rounded to two decimal places. The minimum fee for a single payment is 0.1 yuan; any amount below 0.1 yuan is calculated as 0.1 yuan. The payer must pay the fee. Transaction fees are distributed at a ratio of 8:1:1.</span>
* 链上交易费的80%用于奖励铸块的全节点，10%支付给国储会手续费账户用于社区建设和国储会节点运营，10%转入安全基金账户；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">80% of on-chain transaction fees are used to reward the full node that minted the block, 10% is paid to the National Citizen Reserve Committee fee account for community building and National Citizen Reserve Committee node operations, and 10% is transferred into the security fund account.</span>
* 链下交易上链的，除省储行权益节点获得的手续费外，其链下交易的金额不再支付链上交易费，例：小李支付1000元公民币给小习，由广东省储行节点验证，其费率为0.1%，那么小李需要支付1001元，小习收到1000元，广东省储行节点收到1元，打包该交易上链时，那1000元不用支付链上交易费，而广东省储行获得的1元需要支付链上交易手续费。
  <span class="whitepaper-en">When an off-chain transaction is brought on-chain, the off-chain transaction amount no longer pays an on-chain transaction fee, except for the fee received by the Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank stake node. For example, if Xiao Li pays 1,000 Citizen Coins to Xiao Xi and the transaction is validated by the Guangdong Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank node at a 0.1% rate, Xiao Li pays 1,001 yuan, Xiao Xi receives 1,000 yuan, and the Guangdong Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank node receives 1 yuan. When this transaction is packaged on-chain, the 1,000 yuan does not pay an on-chain transaction fee, but the 1 yuan received by the Guangdong Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank does pay an on-chain transaction fee.</span>

### 5.5.2.链下交易
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">5.5.2. offchain-transaction</span>

* 链下交易手续费由各省储行节点自行设定，可设定的范围为0.01%至0.1%，精度为四舍五入后的小数点后两位，且单笔支付手续费最少为0.01元，不足0.01元的以0.01元计算，链下交易手续费由执行清算的省储行节点所有；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">Off-chain transaction fees are set independently by each Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank node within the range of 0.01% to 0.1%, rounded to two decimal places. The minimum fee for a single payment is 0.01 yuan; any amount below 0.01 yuan is calculated as 0.01 yuan. Off-chain transaction fees belong to the Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank node that performs the clearing.</span>
* 链下交易手续费由付款用户承担，根据收款方所使用的省储行清算费率在付款时同时支付，收款用户每年可更换一次清算省储行；
  <span class="whitepaper-en">The paying user bears the off-chain transaction fee and pays it at the time of payment according to the clearing rate of the Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank used by the recipient. The recipient may change the clearing Provincial Citizen Reserve Bank once per year.</span>
* 链下交易手续费完全由负责清算的省储行节点所有，收入到各省储行多签名治理账户（primitives/src/shengbank_nodes_const.rs/main_address）；

## 6.3.身份识别码系统
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">6.3. sfid</span>

The identity IDs of citizens and natural persons must be generated from record codes. Accordingly, the real identity information of individuals shall be stored offline within the Passport Management Systems independently deployed by municipal public security bureaus, while verifiable unified identity IDs shall be provided by the online Identity Identification Code System, thereby ensuring that personal identity information is not disclosed while still enabling individuals to participate in the Internet using verified real-name identities.</span>

## 6.4.护照管理系统
<span class="whitepaper-heading-en">6.4. cpms</span>

  <span class="whitepaper-en">An independent, offline Citizen Passport Management System shall serve as the sole authoritative source of citizens’ real identities and shall maintain complete identity records for all citizens. The system shall adopt a fully isolated, offline, decentralized deployment architecture, with each municipal public security bureau independently deploying and operating its own Citizen Passport Management System. After a citizen is issued a passport, a record QR code shall be generated and issued. The record code must not contain any personally identifiable information of the citizen and shall be generated using encryption and anonymization technologies. The record code shall be used to generate an identity ID within the Identity Identification Code System. This identity ID shall function as the citizen’s networked digital identity and may be used for purposes such as blockchain voting.</span>
