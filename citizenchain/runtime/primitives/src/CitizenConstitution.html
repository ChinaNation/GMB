第一章 总则 Chapter I General Principles

第一节 国家的定义 Section 1 Definition of the State

第一条 Article 1

先有人类后有国家，是公民建立国家，国家是公民的国家，是公民治理国家，而不是国家统治公民，公民没有爱国的义务；国家政权的建立其基本原则是保护公民的生命权、自由权、财产权、反抗压迫权和选举与被选举权不受任何的非法侵犯，当国家政权无法保证这一基本原则时，公民有权有义务推翻这个政权，建立一个以保障公民生命权、自由权、财产权、反抗压迫权和选举与被选举权为基本原则的政权。 Human beings precede the State. It is citizens who establish the State; the State is the citizens’ State, and it is citizens who govern the State. The State does not rule over citizens, and citizens have no duty to love the State. The establishment of state power shall be founded upon the fundamental principle of protecting citizens’ rights to life, liberty, property, resistance against oppression, and to vote and stand for election from any unlawful infringement. When state power is unable to guarantee this fundamental principle, citizens have the right and the duty to overthrow such power and to establish a government founded upon the fundamental principle of safeguarding citizens’ rights to life, liberty, property, resistance against oppression, and to vote and stand for election.

第二条 Article 2

中华民族联邦共和国是生活在中华民族联邦共和国领土上的所有公民，为了自由、民主、平等及和谐的生活而共同组建的国家，国家政权建立的根本目的是保障所有公民的合法权利不受侵犯，且对于公民的私权，法无禁止皆可为。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation is a state jointly established by all citizens living within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation for the purpose of a free, democratic, equal, and harmonious life. The fundamental purpose of establishing state power is to safeguard all citizens’ lawful rights from infringement; and with respect to citizens’ private rights, whatever is not prohibited by law shall be permitted.

第三条 Article 3

中华民族联邦共和国国家名称是基于中华各民族悠久历史与璀璨文化的沉淀，全称为：中华民族联邦共和国，简称为：中华联邦；中华民族联邦共和国是致力于推行公民治理国家（民治）、实现民主共和（民主）、保障公民权利（民权）、建设民生社会（民生）、复兴民族文化（民族）——的联邦制共和国。 The name of the Federal Republic of the China Nation is founded upon the long history and splendid cultures of the various ethnic groups of the China Nation. Its full name is the Federal Republic of the China Nation, and its abbreviated name is the China Federation. The Federal Republic of the China Nation is a federal republic committed to promoting citizen self-governance, namely governance of the State by citizens; realizing democratic republicanism; safeguarding citizens’ rights; building a society of citizens’ livelihood security; and reviving ethnic cultures.

第四条 Article 4

中华民族联邦共和国国家格言为：我们自由、我们民主、我们正义、我们平等、我们求实、我们勤劳、我们包容，我们是人类的灯塔，加入我们吧！我们的国家意志之一，便是推动人类实现普世价值、实现人人平等、实现人权自由。 The national motto of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be: We are free, we are democratic, we are just, we are equal, we seek truth, we are diligent, and we are inclusive. We are a beacon of humanity. Join us! One of the wills of our State is to advance the realization by humanity of universal values, equality for all, and human rights and freedoms.

第五条 Article 5

中华民族联邦共和国国旗为红地国土自由花，国歌为《中华联邦颂》，国徽为金盾龙凤大好河山，国家官方语言为汉语普通话，官方文字为汉语正体字，国家法定纸质货币为纸质公民币，法定数字货币为数字公民币。 The national flag of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the Freedom Flower Blooming upon the Red Homeland; the national anthem shall be Ode to the China Federation; the national emblem shall be the Golden Shield, Dragon and Phoenix, and the Splendid Homeland; the official language of the State shall be Standard Mandarin Chinese; the official script shall be Traditional Chinese characters; the statutory paper currency of the State shall be the paper Citizen Coin; and the statutory digital currency shall be the digital Citizen Coin.

第六条 Article 6

中华民族联邦共和国领土是固有的疆域，是全体公民赖以生存的家园，非经国家立法院以特别案表决通过的，不得减少任何领土；国家领土增加的，由国家立法院以重要案表决通过。 The sovereign territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation is its inherent domain and the homeland upon which all citizens depend for their existence. No territory may be reduced unless passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a special bill vote; any increase of national territory shall be passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote.

第一款 中华民族联邦共和国领土包括但不限于土地、河流、湖泊、草原、沙漠、山川、戈壁、海域、空域、海外领土和星外领土等本国行使主权的任何区域。 Paragraph 1. The sovereign territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation includes, but is not limited to, any area over which the State exercises sovereignty, including land, rivers, lakes, grasslands, deserts, mountains and rivers, the Gobi, maritime areas, airspace, overseas territories, and extraterrestrial territories.

第二款 中华民族联邦共和国领土分为私有和公有两种类型，私有类型的仅自然人或私法人所能拥有，公有类型的仅公法人所能拥有。 Paragraph 2. The sovereign territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be divided into two types: private ownership and public ownership. Territory under private ownership may be owned only by natural persons or private legal persons; territory under public ownership may be owned only by public legal persons.

第三款 自然人、私法人或公法人取得领土所有权的，由本宪法授权联邦立法院另立法规定。 Paragraph 3. The acquisition of ownership of sovereign territory by natural persons, private legal persons, or public legal persons shall be separately provided by federal legislation authorized by this Constitution.

第七条 Article 7

中华民族联邦共和国依宪法享有行政权、立法权、司法权、监察权、教育权、军事权、外交权、铸币权和征税权等国家权力，简称国家公权或公权，行使公权的机构为国家公权机构，简称公权机构。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall, pursuant to this Constitution, possess state powers including executive power, legislative power, judicial power, control power, education power, military power, diplomatic power, the power of coinage, and the power of taxation. Such powers shall be referred to as the public power of the State, or simply public power. Institutions that exercise public power shall be public-power institutions.

第一款 国家的所有权力均来自于公民的授权，公民通过本宪法及其所属法律将权力赋予公权机构，行权由公权机构的法定代表人依法代表公权机构依法行权，公权机构的法定代表人可将其代表的权力依法授予其他公权机构和公职人员。 Paragraph 1. All powers of the State derive from authorization by citizens. Citizens confer power upon public-power institutions through this Constitution and the laws subordinate to it. Public-power institutions shall exercise power in accordance with law through their legal representatives, who shall represent such institutions in the lawful exercise of power. The legal representative of a public-power institution may, in accordance with law, delegate the power represented by such representative to other public-power institutions and public officials.

第二款 对于代表公权机构行使公权的公职人员，法无授权皆不可为，且任何机构任何人皆不得行使未经公民授权或授权未被公民所知晓及明白的公权。 Paragraph 2. For public officials who exercise public power on behalf of public-power institutions, nothing may be done without authorization by law; and no institution or person may exercise any public power that has not been authorized by citizens, or whose authorization is not known to and understood by citizens.

第八条 Article 8

中华民族联邦共和国的国家权力机构由“一府两会三院”组成，“一府两会三院”分别为政府、公民教育委员会、公民储备委员会、立法院、司法院和监察院，“一府两会三院”相互之间没有隶属关系，其职权独立行使。 The public-power institutions of the State of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall consist of “One Government, Two Committees, and Three Yuan.” The “One Government, Two Committees, and Three Yuan” shall be the Government, the Citizen Education Committee, the Citizen Reserve Committee, the Legislative Yuan, the Judicial Yuan, and the Control Yuan. There shall be no subordinate relationship among the “One Government, Two Committees, and Three Yuan”; each shall exercise its functions and powers independently.

第一款 政府由联邦政府和自治政府组成，联邦政府分为国家联邦政府和省联邦政府，自治政府分为市自治政府和镇自治政府，联邦政府与自治政府之间没有隶属关系。 Paragraph 1. The Government shall consist of the federal government and self-governing governments. The federal government shall be divided into the national federal government and provincial federal governments; self-governing governments shall be divided into municipal self-governing governments and town self-governing governments. There shall be no subordinate relationship between the federal government and self-governing governments.

第二款 国家联邦政府即总统府，省联邦政府简称省政府，省政府是总统府派驻省级行政区的联邦政府机构，省政府隶属于总统府。市自治政府简称市政府，市政府是市级行政区的自治政府机构；镇自治政府简称镇政府，镇政府是市政府派驻镇级行政区的自治政府机构。 Paragraph 2. The national federal government shall be the Presidential Office. A provincial federal government shall be abbreviated as a provincial government. A provincial government is a federal government institution dispatched by the Presidential Office to a provincial administrative region, and shall be subordinate to the Presidential Office. A municipal self-governing government shall be abbreviated as a municipal government, and shall be the self-governing government institution of a municipal administrative region. A town self-governing government shall be abbreviated as a town government, and shall be the self-governing government institution dispatched by a municipal government to a town-level administrative region.

第三款 公民教育委员会简称教委会，教委会由国家教委会、市教委会和校教委会组成，市教委会为市级行政区的教育权行权机构，市教委会与国家教委会之间没有隶属关系；校教委会为各初学、小学、中学和大学的教育权行权机构。 Paragraph 3. The Citizen Education Committee shall be abbreviated as the Education Committee. The Education Committee shall consist of the National Education Committee, municipal education committees, and school education committees. A municipal education committee shall be the institution exercising education power in a municipal administrative region, and there shall be no subordinate relationship between a municipal education committee and the National Education Committee. A school education committee shall be the institution exercising education power in each early learning institution, primary school, secondary school, and university.

第四款 公民储备委员会简称储委会，储委会由国家储备委员会、省储备委员会和省储备银行组成，国家储备委员会、省储备委员会和省储备银行三者相互之间没有隶属关系，公民储备委员会的最高决策机构为公民储备委员会联合会议。 Paragraph 4. The Citizen Reserve Committee shall be abbreviated as the Reserve Committee. The Reserve Committee shall consist of the National Reserve Committee, provincial reserve committees, and provincial reserve banks. There shall be no subordinate relationship among the National Reserve Committee, provincial reserve committees, and provincial reserve banks. The highest decision-making body of the Citizen Reserve Committee shall be the Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee.

第五款 立法院由联邦立法院和市公民立法委员会组成，联邦立法院分为国家立法院和省立法院，省立法院隶属于国家立法院；市公民立法委员会简称市立法会，市立法会为市级行政区的立法机构，市立法会与联邦立法院之间没有隶属关系。 Paragraph 5. The Legislative Yuan shall consist of the Federal Legislative Yuan and municipal citizen legislative committees. The Federal Legislative Yuan shall be divided into the National Legislative Yuan and provincial Legislative Yuan, and a provincial Legislative Yuan shall be subordinate to the National Legislative Yuan. A municipal citizen legislative committee shall be abbreviated as a municipal legislative council, and shall be the legislative institution of a municipal administrative region. There shall be no subordinate relationship between a municipal legislative council and the Federal Legislative Yuan.

第六款 司法院由联邦司法院和市司法院组成，联邦司法院分为国家司法院和省司法院，省司法院隶属于国家司法院；市司法院为市级行政区的司法机构，市司法院与联邦司法院之间没有隶属关系。 Paragraph 6. The Judicial Yuan shall consist of the Federal Judicial Yuan and municipal Judicial Yuan. The Federal Judicial Yuan shall be divided into the National Judicial Yuan and provincial Judicial Yuan, and a provincial Judicial Yuan shall be subordinate to the National Judicial Yuan. A municipal Judicial Yuan shall be the judicial institution of a municipal administrative region. There shall be no subordinate relationship between a municipal Judicial Yuan and the Federal Judicial Yuan.

第七款 监察院由联邦监察院和市监察院组成，联邦监察院分为国家监察院和省监察院，省监察院隶属于国家监察院；市监察院为市级行政区的监察机构，市监察院与联邦监察院之间没有隶属关系。 Paragraph 7. The Control Yuan shall consist of the Federal Control Yuan and municipal Control Yuan. The Federal Control Yuan shall be divided into the National Control Yuan and provincial Control Yuan, and a provincial Control Yuan shall be subordinate to the National Control Yuan. A municipal Control Yuan shall be the control institution of a municipal administrative region. There shall be no subordinate relationship between a municipal Control Yuan and the Federal Control Yuan.

第八款 中华民族联邦共和国总统府、国家立法院、国家司法院、国家监察院住址设于中枢省，中华民族联邦共和国公民教育委员会住址设于北平省，中华民族联邦共和国公民储备委员会住址设于岭南省。 Paragraph 8. The addresses of the Presidential Office, the National Legislative Yuan, the National Judicial Yuan, and the National Control Yuan of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be located in Zhongshu Province. The address of the Citizen Education Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be located in Beiping Province. The address of the Citizen Reserve Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be located in Lingnan Province.

第九条 Article 9

中华民族联邦共和国国家联邦政府，是国家行政权和征税权的最高行权机构，是军事权和外交权的唯一行权机构，国家联邦政府的军事权、外交权、行政权和征税权由宪法赋予；省级行政区没有军事权和外交权，省级行政区的行政权和征税权由总统府授予省政府；市级行政区没有军事权和外交权，市级行政区的行政权和征税权由宪法赋予市政府。 The national federal government of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the State’s executive power and taxation power, and the sole institution exercising military power and diplomatic power. The military power, diplomatic power, executive power, and taxation power of the national federal government shall be vested by this Constitution. Provincial administrative regions shall have no military power or diplomatic power; the executive power and taxation power of provincial administrative regions shall be conferred by the Presidential Office upon provincial governments. Municipal administrative regions shall have no military power or diplomatic power; the executive power and taxation power of municipal administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in municipal governments.

第十条 Article 10

中华民族联邦共和国国家教委会是国家教育权的最高行权机构，国家教委会的教育权由宪法赋予；市级行政区拥有教育权，市级行政区的教育权由宪法赋予市教委会。 The National Education Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the State’s education power, and the education power of the National Education Committee shall be vested by this Constitution. Municipal administrative regions shall possess education power, and the education power of municipal administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in municipal education committees.

第一款 国家教委会负责所有教育类法案草案的起草与初审和全权负责中学、小学和初学教育内容的制订，大学的教育管理制度和教学内容由各大学自行制订。 Paragraph 1. The National Education Committee shall be responsible for drafting and conducting preliminary review of all draft education bills, and shall have full authority over the formulation of educational content for secondary, primary, and early learning education. The educational management systems and teaching content of universities shall be formulated independently by each university.

第二款 国家教委会现任委员总数不得超过155人，不得少于105人，分别由各省级行政区和各大学联盟选举产生；每个省级行政区限选举产生2名国家教委会委员，大学联盟选举产生的国家教委会委员总数不得超过55人，不得少于35人。 Paragraph 2. The total number of incumbent members of the National Education Committee shall not exceed 155 and shall not be fewer than 105. They shall be elected respectively by provincial administrative regions and university alliances. Each provincial administrative region may elect no more than two members of the National Education Committee. The total number of members of the National Education Committee elected by university alliances shall not exceed 55 and shall not be fewer than 35.

第三款 国家教委会委员中，由大学联盟选举产生的大学历史学教授、教育学教授、法学教授和哲学教授委员各不得少于2名，由省级行政区选举产生的中学、小学和初学教师委员各不得少于10名。 Paragraph 3. Among the members of the National Education Committee, there shall be no fewer than two members each who are professors of history, education, law, and philosophy elected by university alliances, and no fewer than ten members each who are secondary school, primary school, and early learning teachers elected by provincial administrative regions.

第四款 中华民族联邦共和国实行12年制义务教育，12年义务教育由初学3年、小学4年、中学5年组成，公民完成12年义务教育后，可选择休学或进入大学教育阶段，大学教育阶段由学士教育、硕士教育和博士教育组成。 Paragraph 4. The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall implement twelve-year compulsory education. The twelve years of compulsory education shall consist of three years of early learning education, four years of primary education, and five years of secondary education. After completing twelve years of compulsory education, citizens may choose to suspend their studies or enter the stage of university education. University education shall consist of bachelor’s education, master’s education, and doctoral education.

第五款 中华民族联邦共和国义务教育阶段，国语为必修语言，中学义务教育阶段，社会学、司法学和历史学为必修课程。 Paragraph 5. During the compulsory education stage of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, the national language shall be a compulsory language. During the secondary compulsory education stage, sociology, judicial studies, and history shall be compulsory courses.

第十一条 Article 11

中华民族联邦共和国公民储备委员会是铸币权的最高行权机构，公民储备委员会的铸币权由宪法赋予；中华民族联邦共和国国家储备委员会是国家铸币权的行权机构，国家储备委员会的铸币权由宪法赋予；省级行政区拥有铸币权，省级行政区的铸币权由宪法赋予省储备委员会。 The Citizen Reserve Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the power of coinage, and the power of coinage of the Citizen Reserve Committee shall be vested by this Constitution. The National Reserve Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the institution exercising the State’s power of coinage, and the power of coinage of the National Reserve Committee shall be vested by this Constitution. Provincial administrative regions shall possess the power of coinage, and the power of coinage of provincial administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in provincial reserve committees.

第一款 公民储备委员会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位2个，由公民储备委员会委员互选产生，国家储备委员会主席和副主席、国家储备委员会委员、省储备委员会主席和省储备银行行长为公民储备委员会委员。 Paragraph 1. The Citizen Reserve Committee shall have one chairperson position and two vice-chairperson positions, to be elected by and from among the members of the Citizen Reserve Committee. The chairperson and vice-chairpersons of the National Reserve Committee, the members of the National Reserve Committee, the chairpersons of provincial reserve committees, and the governors of provincial reserve banks shall be members of the Citizen Reserve Committee.

第二款 国家储备委员会、省储备委员会和省储备银行在公民储备委员会的领导下依法制订和执行货币政策、依法发行货币、依法监管金融市场和依法管理支付、清算和结算事务。 Paragraph 2. Under the leadership of the Citizen Reserve Committee, the National Reserve Committee, provincial reserve committees, and provincial reserve banks shall, in accordance with law, formulate and implement monetary policy, issue currency, regulate financial markets, and manage payment, clearing, and settlement affairs.

第三款 国家储备委员会由19名委员组成，且同时在职的委员不得少于15人，国家储备委员会委员每届任期15年，不得连任，不得兼任第二职业；国家储备委员会委员必须由总统提名，且非经国家参议会以重要案表决通过的，国家储备委员会委员不得被免职。 Paragraph 3. The National Reserve Committee shall consist of 19 members, and the number of members concurrently in office shall not be fewer than 15. Each term of office for members of the National Reserve Committee shall be 15 years; members may not serve consecutive terms and may not concurrently hold a second occupation. Members of the National Reserve Committee must be nominated by the President, and may not be removed from office unless such removal is passed by the National Senate through a major bill vote.

第四款 省储备委员会由9名委员组成，且同时在职的委员不得少于5人，省储备委员会委员每届任期10年，不得连任，不得兼任第二职业；省储备委员会委员必须由省长提名，且非经省参议会以重要案表决通过的，省储备委员会委员不得被免职。 Paragraph 4. A provincial reserve committee shall consist of nine members, and the number of members concurrently in office shall not be fewer than five. Each term of office for members of a provincial reserve committee shall be ten years; members may not serve consecutive terms and may not concurrently hold a second occupation. Members of a provincial reserve committee must be nominated by the governor of the province, and may not be removed from office unless such removal is passed by the Provincial Senate through a major bill vote.

第五款 每个省级行政区限成立一家公民储备银行，本省省储备委员会及财政与税务厅为该省公民储备银行的当然股东，分别持股5%；省储备银行股东总数不得低于30个，其中，自然人股东不得低于15个，公法人股东不得超过2个，且必须是本省辖区内的法人或自然人。 Paragraph 5. Each provincial administrative region may establish no more than one Citizen Reserve Bank. The provincial reserve committee and the Department of Finance and Taxation of that province shall be ex officio shareholders of the Citizen Reserve Bank of that province, each holding five percent of the shares. The total number of shareholders of a provincial reserve bank shall not be fewer than 30, among whom there shall be no fewer than 15 natural-person shareholders and no more than two public-legal-person shareholders, and all such shareholders must be legal persons or natural persons within the jurisdiction of that province.

第十二条 Article 12

中华民族联邦共和国国家立法院是国家立法权的最高行权机构，国家立法院的立法权由宪法赋予；省级行政区拥有立法权，省级行政区的立法权由国家立法院授予省立法院；市级行政区拥有立法权，市级行政区的立法权由宪法赋予市立法会。 The National Legislative Yuan of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the State’s legislative power, and the legislative power of the National Legislative Yuan shall be vested by this Constitution. Provincial administrative regions shall possess legislative power, and the legislative power of provincial administrative regions shall be conferred by the National Legislative Yuan upon provincial legislative yuan. Municipal administrative regions shall possess legislative power, and the legislative power of municipal administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in municipal legislative councils.

第一款 国家立法院设众议员议政会，简称国家众议会，国家众议会现任众议员总数不得超过355人，不得少于305人；每个省级行政区最少选出5名国家众议会众议员，其省辖区人口在500万人以下的选出5人，其省辖区人口超过500万人的，后每满500万人增选1人，最多可增选至15人，且每个省级行政区同时在任的众议员不得少于3人；国家众议会众议员每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 Paragraph 1. The National Legislative Yuan shall establish a House of Representatives, abbreviated as the National House of Representatives. The total number of incumbent representatives of the National House of Representatives shall not exceed 355 and shall not be fewer than 305. Each provincial administrative region shall elect at least five representatives to the National House of Representatives; a provincial administrative region with a population of fewer than five million within its jurisdiction shall elect five representatives, and where the population within its jurisdiction exceeds five million, one additional representative shall be elected for each additional full five million persons thereafter, up to a total of 15 representatives. Each provincial administrative region shall have no fewer than three representatives concurrently in office. Each term of office for representatives of the National House of Representatives shall be three years, and no representative may serve more than two terms.

第二款 国家立法院设参议员议政会，简称国家参议会，国家参议会现任参议员总数不得超过155人，不得少于105人；每个省级行政区可选出3名国家参议会参议员，且每个省级行政区同时在任的参议员不得少于2人；国家参议会参议员每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 Paragraph 2. The National Legislative Yuan shall establish a Senate, abbreviated as the National Senate. The total number of incumbent senators of the National Senate shall not exceed 155 and shall not be fewer than 105. Each provincial administrative region may elect three senators to the National Senate, and each provincial administrative region shall have no fewer than two senators concurrently in office. Each term of office for senators of the National Senate shall be five years, and no senator may serve more than two terms.

第三款 国家参议会参议员由各省参议会现任参议员互选产生，且担任国家参议会职务的不得再担任国家众议会职务；国家众议会众议员由各省众议会现任众议员互选产生，且担任国家众议会职务的不得再担任国家参议会职务。 Paragraph 3. Senators of the National Senate shall be elected by and from among the incumbent senators of the provincial senates, and a person serving in the National Senate may not concurrently serve in the National House of Representatives. Representatives of the National House of Representatives shall be elected by and from among the incumbent representatives of the provincial houses of representatives, and a person serving in the National House of Representatives may not concurrently serve in the National Senate.

第十三条 Article 13

中华民族联邦共和国国家司法院是国家司法权的最高行权机构，是中华民族联邦共和国法律的最终解释机构，国家司法院的司法权由宪法赋予；省级行政区拥有司法权，省级行政区的司法权由国家司法院授予省司法院；市级行政区拥有司法权，市级行政区的司法权由宪法赋予市司法院。 The National Judicial Yuan of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the State’s judicial power and the final institution for interpreting the laws of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. The judicial power of the National Judicial Yuan shall be vested by this Constitution. Provincial administrative regions shall possess judicial power, and the judicial power of provincial administrative regions shall be conferred by the National Judicial Yuan upon provincial Judicial Yuan. Municipal administrative regions shall possess judicial power, and the judicial power of municipal administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in municipal Judicial Yuan.

第十四条 Article 14

中华民族联邦共和国国家监察院是国家监察权的最高行权机构，国家监察院的监察权由宪法赋予；省级行政区拥有监察权，省级行政区的监察权由国家监察院授予省监察院；市级行政区拥有监察权，市级行政区的监察权由宪法赋予市监察院。 The National Control Yuan of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest institution exercising the State’s control power, and the control power of the National Control Yuan shall be vested by this Constitution. Provincial administrative regions shall possess control power, and the control power of provincial administrative regions shall be conferred by the National Control Yuan upon provincial Control Yuan. Municipal administrative regions shall possess control power, and the control power of municipal administrative regions shall be vested by this Constitution in municipal Control Yuan.

第十五条 Article 15

中华民族联邦共和国的行政区由省、市、镇共三级组成，每个行政区内由“一府两会三院”派驻相应的公权机构和公职人员，各级行政区属于中华民族联邦共和国不可分割的一部分，必须拥护宪法的权威，执行宪法的条款和保障领土的完整及国家主权的独立。 The administrative regions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall consist of three levels: provinces, municipalities, and towns. Within each administrative region, corresponding public-power institutions and public officials shall be dispatched by the “One Government, Two Committees, and Three Yuan.” Administrative regions at all levels shall be inseparable parts of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, and must uphold the authority of the Constitution, implement the provisions of the Constitution, and safeguard territorial integrity and the independence of national sovereignty.

第十六条 Article 16

中华民族联邦共和国公权机构包含行政、立法、司法、监察等机构，增加、减少和变更国家级公权机构的，由国家立法院以特别案表决通过；增加、减少和变更部级公权机构或省级行政区的，由国家立法院以重要案表决通过。 The public-power institutions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall include executive, legislative, judicial, and control institutions, among others. The addition, reduction, or alteration of national-level public-power institutions shall be passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a special bill vote. The addition, reduction, or alteration of ministerial-level public-power institutions or provincial administrative regions shall be passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote.

第一款 省级行政区减少和变更所辖市级行政区的，由省立法院和该市立法会以重要案表决通过；省级行政区增加所辖市级行政区的，由省立法院和拟成立市的临时市立法会以重要案表决通过。 Paragraph 1. The reduction or alteration by a provincial administrative region of municipal administrative regions under its jurisdiction shall be passed by the provincial Legislative Yuan and the municipal legislative council of the municipality concerned through a major bill vote. The addition by a provincial administrative region of a municipal administrative region under its jurisdiction shall be passed by the provincial Legislative Yuan and the temporary municipal legislative council of the proposed municipality through a major bill vote.

第二款 市级行政区变更和减少所辖镇级行政区的，由该镇自治会提案，市立法会以重要案表决通过；市级行政区增加所辖镇级行政区的，由拟成立镇的临时镇自治会提案，市立法会以重要案表决通过。 Paragraph 2. The alteration or reduction by a municipal administrative region of town-level administrative regions under its jurisdiction shall be proposed by the Town Self-Governing Committee concerned and passed by the municipal legislative council through a major bill vote. The addition by a municipal administrative region of a town-level administrative region under its jurisdiction shall be proposed by the Temporary Town Self-Governing Committee of the proposed town and passed by the municipal legislative council through a major bill vote.

第三款 增加、减少和变更其他公权机构或其他行政区的，由各立法机构另立法规定。 Paragraph 3. The addition, reduction, or alteration of other public-power institutions or other administrative regions shall be separately provided by the respective legislative institutions through legislation.

第二节 宪法的定义 Section 2 Definition of the Constitution

第十七条 Article 17

我们是中华民族联邦共和国的公民，为建立一个自由的、民主的、正义的、平等的、求实的国家，为保障公民权利、保障社会福利、保障国家安全，使我们和我们的后代能自由、平等、幸福、健康的生活在这个国家，特为中华民族联邦共和国制定《中华民族联邦共和国宪法》，简称宪法、公民宪法或公民宪章。 We are citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. In order to establish a free, democratic, just, equal, and truth-seeking State; to safeguard citizens’ rights, social welfare, and national security; and to enable us and our descendants to live freely, equally, happily, and healthily in this State, we hereby enact the Constitution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, abbreviated as the Constitution, the Citizen Constitution, or the Citizen Charter.

第十八条 Article 18

本宪法是中华民族联邦共和国的根本法，是国家所有法律的基础，一切违反本宪法的法律都不是法律，宪法应删除取缔一切与本宪法相抵触的法律；如有违宪法的决议，公民有权不予执行，如有违宪法的行为，公民有义务向国家护宪大法官检举。 This Constitution is the fundamental law of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and the foundation of all laws of the State. Any law that violates this Constitution shall have no force of law, and all laws in conflict with this Constitution shall be repealed and abolished pursuant to this Constitution. Where a resolution violates the Constitution, citizens shall have the right not to execute it; where an act violates the Constitution, citizens shall have the duty to report it to the National Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices.

第一款 本宪法赋予的最高立法权，属于中华民族联邦共和国国家立法院，国家立法院依宪法授予省立法院立法权，省立法院的立法不得与宪法冲突，不得与国家立法院的立法冲突。 Paragraph 1. The highest legislative power vested by this Constitution shall belong to the National Legislative Yuan of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. The National Legislative Yuan shall confer legislative power upon provincial Legislative Yuan pursuant to the Constitution. Legislation enacted by a provincial Legislative Yuan shall not conflict with the Constitution or with legislation enacted by the National Legislative Yuan.

第二款 本宪法赋予中华民族联邦共和国市级行政区市立法会立法权，市立法会的立法权不应超出所属市级行政区的辖区范围，且市立法会的立法不得与宪法冲突，不得与国家立法院的立法冲突，不应与省立法院的立法冲突。 Paragraph 2. This Constitution shall vest legislative power in the municipal legislative councils of municipal administrative regions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. The legislative power of a municipal legislative council shall not exceed the territorial scope of the municipal administrative region to which it belongs, and legislation enacted by a municipal legislative council shall not conflict with the Constitution or with legislation enacted by the National Legislative Yuan, and should not conflict with legislation enacted by a provincial Legislative Yuan.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国宪法赋予立法机构的立法权，其立法理念应建立在以人为本的基础之上，立法的目的不是惩罚与管制，立法的根本目的是教育与防范，是教恶扬善和防止犯罪；且宪法未尽之法律，由各立法院和立法会另立法规定。 Paragraph 3. The legislative power vested by the Constitution in legislative institutions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be exercised upon a people-oriented legislative philosophy. The purpose of legislation is not punishment and control; the fundamental purpose of legislation is education and prevention, namely to educate against evil conduct, promote good conduct, and prevent crime. Matters of law not fully provided by the Constitution shall be separately provided by each Legislative Yuan and legislative council through legislation.

第十九条 Article 19

本宪法的第一章总则是宪法的核心，对宪法核心章节条款进行修改的，必须经国家立法院以特别案表决通过，且不得对本宪法的第一条、第二条、第三条、第十七条、第十九条、第二十三条、第三十三条和第四十一条进行修改；对本宪法除第一章总则以外的其它章节条款进行修改的，必须经国家立法院以重要案表决通过。 Chapter I, General Principles, of this Constitution shall be the core of the Constitution. Any amendment to the provisions of the core chapter of the Constitution must be passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a special bill vote, and Articles 1, 2, 3, 17, 19, 23, 33, and 41 of this Constitution shall not be amended. Any amendment to provisions of chapters of this Constitution other than Chapter I, General Principles, must be passed by the National Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote.

第二十条 Article 20

为维护宪法的神圣权威，保护宪法的不受侵犯，特设护宪大法官7人；护宪大法官每届任期6年，连任超过5届的，从第6届起授予终身护宪大法官荣誉称谓；中华民族联邦共和国的违宪审查权属于护宪大法官集体所有，经护宪大法官多数通过的，可指定任意司法院对公民、公职人员和军队军人发起违宪审查。 In order to uphold the sacred authority of the Constitution and protect the Constitution from infringement, seven Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices are hereby established. Each term of office of a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice shall be six years. A Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice who is re-elected for more than five terms shall, beginning with the sixth term, be granted the honorary title of Lifetime Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice. The power of constitutional review of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall belong collectively to the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices. Upon approval by a majority of the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices, any Judicial Yuan may be designated to initiate constitutional review against citizens, public officials, and military personnel.

第一款 护宪大法官的基本任职要求：一是拥有专业的司法学识和丰富的司法经验，且在司法领域作出重大突出贡献或参与主审对司法界影响重大的案件；二是人品正直极富正义感，不畏强权能始终坚守法律的公平与正义；三是以身作则维护法律的尊严，深受公民的爱戴与认可。 Paragraph 1. The basic qualifications for office as a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice shall be: first, possessing professional judicial knowledge and extensive judicial experience, and having made major and outstanding contributions in the judicial field or participated as a presiding judge in cases of major influence upon the judiciary; second, being upright in character and endowed with a strong sense of justice, not fearing power and always upholding the fairness and justice of law; and third, setting an example in safeguarding the dignity of law and being deeply loved, respected, and recognized by citizens.

第二款 护宪大法官不隶属于任何机构，其职权由宪法直接赋予，只对宪法负责；非不可抗力的因素或任期届满的，护宪大法官的任职，不得被剥夺。 Paragraph 2. Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall not be subordinate to any institution. Their functions and powers shall be directly vested by the Constitution, and they shall be responsible only to the Constitution. Except by reason of force majeure or expiration of term, the office of a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice shall not be deprived.

第三款 护宪大法官由拥有选举权的公民选举产生，任职由任意在职护宪大法官主持，护宪大法官宣誓就职的，身着护宪大法官服，左手抚中华民族联邦共和国宪法，右手持护宪大法官权杖宣誓。 Paragraph 3. Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall be elected by citizens possessing the right to vote. The assumption of office shall be presided over by any incumbent Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice. When a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice takes the oath of office, the Justice shall wear the robe of a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice, place the left hand upon the Constitution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, and hold the scepter of a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice in the right hand while taking the oath.

第四款 护宪大法官就职誓词：我是中华民族联邦共和国公民，我宣誓就职中华民族联邦共和国护宪大法官，我在此宣誓！我将以超过对待自己生命权的高度对待我所任职的护宪大法官职权，我保证我曾经的正义感、使命感和光荣感会在此之后更加的得到体现！我在此庄严承诺！我将任职、守护宪法，与一切违反宪法的行为进行战斗！ Paragraph 4. The oath of office of a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice shall be: “I am a citizen of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. I swear to assume office as a Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. I hereby swear that I shall treat the functions and powers of the office of Constitution-Guarding Grand Justice with a degree of solemnity exceeding that with which I treat my own right to life. I pledge that my sense of justice, mission, and honor shall be manifested even more strongly from this day forward. I hereby solemnly undertake that I shall hold office, guard the Constitution, and fight against all acts that violate the Constitution.”

第二十一条 Article 21

在总统府总统或副总统、国家立法院院长或副院长、国家参议会参议长或副参议长、国家众议会众议长或副众议长、国家司法院院长或副院长、国家监察院院长或副院长、联邦廉政署署长或副署长、联邦审计署署长或副署长、联邦调查署署长或副署长、国家教委会主席或副主席、国家储备委员会主席或副主席和公民储备委员会主席或副主席出现违宪时，国家护宪大法官有权有义务直接发起违宪审查。 Where the President or Vice President of the Presidential Office, the President or Vice President of the National Legislative Yuan, the Speaker or Deputy Speaker of the National Senate, the Speaker or Deputy Speaker of the National House of Representatives, the President or Vice President of the National Judicial Yuan, the President or Vice President of the National Control Yuan, the Director or Deputy Director of the Federal Integrity Agency, the Director or Deputy Director of the Federal Audit Agency, the Director or Deputy Director of the Federal Investigation Agency, the chairperson or vice-chairperson of the National Education Committee, the chairperson or vice-chairperson of the National Reserve Committee, or the chairperson or vice-chairperson of the Citizen Reserve Committee commits a constitutional violation, the National Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall have the right and the duty to directly initiate constitutional review.

第二十二条 Article 22

在军队军人出现违宪时，联邦司法院有权有义务发起军队军人违宪指控听证会，军队军人有义务出席违宪指控听证会，根据听证会结论，联邦司法院可向护宪大法官提请升级军队军人违宪指控为军队军人违宪审查。 Where military personnel commit a constitutional violation, the Federal Judicial Yuan shall have the right and the duty to initiate a hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality against such military personnel. Military personnel shall have the duty to attend the hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality. Based on the conclusion of the hearing, the Federal Judicial Yuan may petition the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices to elevate the allegations of unconstitutionality against military personnel into constitutional review of military personnel.

第三节 军队的定义 Section 3 Definition of the Armed Forces

第二十三条 Article 23

军队是国家军事权的具体体现，军队的基本职责有以下三项：一是维护公民的合法权利，保障公民的人权不受侵犯、保障公民的财产不受侵犯；二是维护国家领土的完整，保障国家领土不受侵犯、保障国家主权不受侵犯；三是维护世界和平，保障人类的生存权不受侵犯、保障人类的人权不受侵犯。 The armed forces are the concrete embodiment of the State’s military power. The basic duties of the armed forces shall consist of the following three items: first, to protect the lawful rights of citizens, to safeguard citizens’ human rights from infringement, and to safeguard citizens’ property from infringement; second, to protect the integrity of national territory, to safeguard national territory from infringement, and to safeguard national sovereignty from infringement; and third, to maintain world peace, to safeguard humanity’s right to survival from infringement, and to safeguard human rights of humanity from infringement.

第二十四条 Article 24

中华民族联邦共和国总统府总统是中华民族联邦共和国军队的最高统帅，由宪法授权行使国家军事权，统领国家军队，总统有权解除任意一名军人的军衔和职务，有权解散任意一支军队的武装；且国家处于战争状态或紧急状态时，非不可抗力的因素，现任总统的军事统帅权不得被剥夺。 The President of the Presidential Office of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the supreme commander of the armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. The President shall be authorized by this Constitution to exercise the State’s military power and command the national armed forces. The President shall have the power to remove the military rank and office of any member of the armed forces and to disarm any armed force. Where the State is in a state of war or a state of emergency, the military command authority of the incumbent President shall not be deprived except by reason of force majeure.

第二十五条 Article 25

中华民族联邦共和国国家防务部是中华民族联邦共和国的最高军事管理机构，下设陆军部、海军部、空军部、天军部和联合作战参谋部；中华民族联邦共和国军队由陆军、海军、空军、天军和国民警卫队共五个军种组成，陆军、海军、空军和天军分别由国家防务部所属陆军部、海军部、空军部和天军部管理，国民警卫队由国土安全部所属国民警卫局管理。 The Department of Defense of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest military administrative institution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, and shall have under it the Department of the Army, the Department of the Navy, the Department of the Air Force, the Department of the Space Force, and the Joint Operations Staff. The armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall consist of five services: the Army, the Navy, the Air Force, the Space Force, and the National Guard. The Army, the Navy, the Air Force, and the Space Force shall be administered respectively by the Department of the Army, the Department of the Navy, the Department of the Air Force, and the Department of the Space Force under the Department of Defense. The National Guard shall be administered by the National Guard Bureau under the Department of Homeland Security.

第二十六条 Article 26

中华民族联邦共和国国家防务部联合作战参谋部，是中华民族联邦共和国的最高军事作战机构；联合作战参谋部设总参谋长职位1个，设副总参谋长职位1个，由总统提名并任免产生，对总统负责；设参谋长职位5个，分别由陆军司令员、海军司令员、空军司令员、天军司令员和国民警卫队司令员兼任。 The Joint Operations Staff of the Department of Defense of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest military operations institution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. The Joint Operations Staff shall have one position of Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff and one position of Vice Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, both to be nominated, appointed, and removed by the President, and both shall be responsible to the President. The Joint Operations Staff shall have five Chief of Staff positions, to be held concurrently by the Commander of the Army, the Commander of the Navy, the Commander of the Air Force, the Commander of the Space Force, and the Commander of the National Guard, respectively.

第二十七条 Article 27

中华民族联邦共和国陆军、海军、空军、天军和国民警卫队，分别设陆军司令部、海军司令部、空军司令部、天军司令部和国民警卫队司令部，每个军种司令部设司令员职位1个，由联合作战参谋部总参谋长提名，总统任免产生；每个军种司令部设副司令员职位2个，由本军种司令部司令员提名，联合作战参谋部总参谋长任免产生。 The Army, Navy, Air Force, Space Force, and National Guard of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall respectively establish the Army Command, Navy Command, Air Force Command, Space Force Command, and National Guard Command. Each service command shall have one Commander position, to be nominated by the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff and appointed or removed by the President. Each service command shall have two Deputy Commander positions, to be nominated by the Commander of that service command and appointed or removed by the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff.

第二十八条 Article 28

中华民族联邦共和国军队军人在未取得公民的同意下，禁止在非战争状态或非紧急状态时期进入公民的私人领地；在战争状态或紧急状态时期，军队军人有权进入公民的私人领地，有权征用公民的财产，因征用使用而造成公民财产损失的，军队应予以补偿。 Military personnel of the armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be prohibited from entering citizens’ private territory during periods that are neither a state of war nor a state of emergency without the consent of the citizens concerned. During a state of war or a state of emergency, military personnel shall have the right to enter citizens’ private territory and to requisition citizens’ property. Where loss is caused to citizens’ property as a result of requisition and use, the armed forces shall provide compensation.

第二十九条 Article 29

中华民族联邦共和国军队是国家的军队、是公民的军队，禁止军队军人为任何政党、宗教和非法组织提供武力保护，在政治事务中，军队军人必须保持中立。 The armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation are the armed forces of the State and the armed forces of the citizens. Military personnel shall be prohibited from providing armed protection for any political party, religion, or illegal organization. In political affairs, military personnel must remain neutral.

第三十条 Article 30

中华民族联邦共和国现役职业军人禁止经商，禁止兼任政府、立法院、司法院、监察院、公民教育委员会和公民储备委员会等国家公权机构的公职职务，禁止参加任何政党或政治团体。 Active-duty professional military personnel of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be prohibited from engaging in business, from concurrently holding public offices in state public-power institutions including the Government, the Legislative Yuan, the Judicial Yuan, the Control Yuan, the Citizen Education Committee, and the Citizen Reserve Committee, and from joining any political party or political organization.

第三十一条 Article 31

中华民族联邦共和国军队军人违宪的，国家立法院有权通过重要案表决宣布其为非法武装，总统有权签署总统令解除其武装，国家立法院通过重要案表决宣布其为非法武装的，公民有权解除其武装。 Where military personnel of the armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation violate the Constitution, the National Legislative Yuan shall have the power to declare them an illegal armed force through a major bill vote, the President shall have the power to issue a presidential order to disarm them, and where the National Legislative Yuan declares them an illegal armed force through a major bill vote, citizens shall have the right to disarm them.

第三十二条 Article 32

中华民族联邦共和国军事机构在没有总统的授权下，不得在本国境内非军事区开展军事行动；在非战争状态或非紧急状态下，军事机构在本国境内非军事区开展军事行动的，必须有联邦调查署所属公职人员的参与。 Military institutions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall not conduct military operations in non-military zones within the territory of the State without authorization from the President. During periods that are neither a state of war nor a state of emergency, military institutions conducting military operations in non-military zones within the territory of the State must have the participation of public officials belonging to the Federal Investigation Agency.

第四节 公民的权利与义务 Section 4 Rights and Duties of Citizens

第三十三条 Article 33

人自出生即是自由的、平等的、不受压迫的，凡中华民族联邦共和国公民，无论肤色、种族、民族、宗教、性别、地域、党派、职业、性取向和健康状况，其在法律上一律平等，且公民的人权大于国家的主权。 Human beings are free, equal, and free from oppression from birth. All citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, regardless of skin color, race, ethnicity, religion, sex, region, party affiliation, occupation, sexual orientation, or health condition, shall be equal before the law, and citizens’ human rights shall take precedence over the sovereignty of the State.

第三十四条 Article 34

中华民族联邦共和国公民是指拥有本国国籍的自然人，本国国籍是指由中华民族联邦共和国公民安全局颁发的中华民族联邦共和国护照，自然人是指性别为男性或女性的智人；年满十六周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民为成年人，未满十六周岁的为未成年人，未成年人的权利与义务由其监护人负责。 Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall mean natural persons possessing the nationality of this State. The nationality of this State shall mean the passport of the Federal Republic of the China Nation issued by the Citizen Security Bureau of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. A natural person shall mean a Homo sapiens human being whose sex is male or female. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained sixteen years of age shall be adults, and those who have not attained sixteen years of age shall be minors. The rights and duties of minors shall be the responsibility of their guardians.

第一款 取得中华民族联邦共和国护照，需向中华民族联邦共和国公民安全局下设的任意分支机构提出入籍申请，入籍后由公民安全局颁发中华民族联邦共和国护照。 Paragraph 1. To obtain a passport of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, a naturalization application shall be submitted to any branch institution under the Citizen Security Bureau of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. After naturalization, the Citizen Security Bureau shall issue a passport of the Federal Republic of the China Nation.

第二款 出生在中华民族联邦共和国领土上的自然人，出生即拥有本国国籍；出生在非中华民族联邦共和国领土上的自然人，其亲生父母有一方是中华民族联邦共和国公民的，出生即拥有本国国籍。 Paragraph 2. A natural person born within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall possess the nationality of this State from birth. A natural person born outside the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall possess the nationality of this State from birth if either biological parent is a citizen of the Federal Republic of the China Nation.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国公民享有自由选择国籍和选择多国国籍的权利，拥有本国国籍的，在入境本国及在本国境内时必须遵守本国法律和使用本国护照。 Paragraph 3. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall enjoy the right freely to choose nationality and to choose multiple nationalities. Those possessing the nationality of this State must comply with the laws of this State and use the passport of this State when entering this State and while within the territory of this State.

第四款 中华民族联邦共和国公民入籍誓词：我宣誓！遵守中华民族联邦共和国宪法及其附属法律、认同先有人类后有国家，是公民建立国家，国家是公民的国家，是公民治理国家，而不是国家统治公民的国家概念、认同公民有依法纳税、依法服兵役、依法受教育和依法保护环境的义务。也认同，当法律要求时，我愿意为自由民主而拿起武器、为捍卫正义而拿起武器、为平等人权而拿起武器、为保卫领土而拿起武器、为守护宪法而拿起武器。我在此宣誓，对以上誓言绝无借口、谎言、障碍与保留！ Paragraph 4. The naturalization oath of citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be: “I swear to observe the Constitution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and the laws subordinate to it; to recognize the concept of the State that human beings precede the State, that citizens establish the State, that the State is the citizens’ State, and that citizens govern the State, rather than the State ruling citizens; and to recognize that citizens have the duties to pay taxes in accordance with law, perform military service in accordance with law, receive education in accordance with law, and protect the environment in accordance with law. I also recognize that, when required by law, I am willing to take up arms for freedom and democracy, to take up arms to defend justice, to take up arms for equal human rights, to take up arms to defend the territory, and to take up arms to guard the Constitution. I hereby swear that there shall be no excuse, falsehood, obstacle, or reservation with respect to the foregoing oath.”

第三十五条 Article 35

中华民族联邦共和国公民依宪法享有言论自由、信仰自由、迁居自由、生育自由、结社自由、出版自由、隐私通信自由、免于恐惧的自由和不受饥饿的自由等自由的权利，非经司法院依照现行法律审判的，不得剥夺公民的上述权利。 Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall, pursuant to the Constitution, enjoy freedoms including freedom of speech, freedom of belief, freedom of relocation, reproductive freedom, freedom of association, freedom of publication, freedom of private communication, freedom from fear, and freedom from hunger. The foregoing rights of citizens shall not be deprived except by adjudication of a Judicial Yuan in accordance with current law.

第一款 中华民族联邦共和国公民享有生存权、工作权、财产权、诉讼权及考取公职权，非经联邦司法院终审的，不得剥夺公民的上述权利。 Paragraph 1. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall enjoy the right to survival, the right to work, property rights, the right of litigation, and the right to take examinations for public office. The foregoing rights of citizens shall not be deprived except by final adjudication of the Federal Judicial Yuan.

第二款 中华民族联邦共和国公民年满十六周岁的，即拥有选举权与被选举权，即可依法参与公职人员的选举与被选举；公民参与选举的投票权不得用于买卖交易，凡买卖交易投票权的，经联邦司法院审判违宪的，参与投票权买卖交易的公民永久不得再参与公职人员的选举与被选举。 Paragraph 2. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained sixteen years of age shall possess the right to vote and the right to stand for election, and may participate in the election of public officials as voters or candidates in accordance with law. A citizen’s voting right in elections shall not be used for purchase or sale transactions. Any citizen who participates in the purchase or sale of voting rights shall, if adjudicated unconstitutional by the Federal Judicial Yuan, be permanently prohibited from again participating in the election of public officials as a voter or candidate.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国公民享有合法拥有财产的权利，包括但不限于土地、房屋、货币、股权、债券和知识产权等，非经联邦司法院终审的，不得剥夺公民的上述权利。 Paragraph 3. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall enjoy the right lawfully to own property, including but not limited to land, houses, currency, equity interests, bonds, and intellectual property. The foregoing rights of citizens shall not be deprived except by final adjudication of the Federal Judicial Yuan.

第四款 中华民族联邦共和国公民，年满二十二周岁且获得公民义务教育结业证书的，没有医学精神类疾病的，具有独立行为能力的，十年内没有刑事犯罪记录的，依宪法享有合法拥有枪弹武器的权利。 Paragraph 4. Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained twenty-two years of age, have obtained a certificate of completion of compulsory citizen education, have no medical psychiatric illness, possess independent capacity for conduct, and have no criminal record within the preceding ten years shall, pursuant to the Constitution, enjoy the right lawfully to own firearms, ammunition, and weapons.

第五款 中华民族联邦共和国公民的病例资料、通信资料、征信资料、银行资料、档案资料和家庭住址属于公民的绝对隐私，非经公民本人同意或非经市司法院和省司法院双重授权的，不得泄漏和使用公民的上述隐私。 Paragraph 5. The medical records, communications data, credit information, banking information, archival records, and home addresses of citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be citizens’ absolute privacy. The foregoing privacy of citizens shall not be disclosed or used except with the consent of the citizen concerned or upon dual authorization by a municipal Judicial Yuan and a provincial Judicial Yuan.

第六款 中华民族联邦共和国公民的其他自由权利，在不违反现行法律、不妨碍社会秩序及不损害公共利益时，均受宪法的保护。 Paragraph 6. Other freedoms and rights of citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be protected by the Constitution when they do not violate current law, obstruct social order, or impair the public interest.

第三十六条 Article 36

中华民族联邦共和国公民非经司法院依照现行法律判定有罪的，不得被关押，且遭受非法逮捕、拘禁、审问、处罚的，公民有权予以拒绝。公民因违法嫌疑需要被临时逮捕拘禁的，其执行机构需出示由监察院出具的《违法嫌疑人拘捕通知》。公民除依法服现役外，不受军事法庭的审判，公民依法服现役的，除违宪和廉政违法外，不受司法院的审判。 Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall not be detained unless found guilty by a Judicial Yuan in accordance with current law, and citizens subjected to unlawful arrest, detention, interrogation, or punishment shall have the right to refuse it. Where a citizen must be temporarily arrested or detained on suspicion of violating the law, the executing institution must present a Notice of Arrest and Detention of a Suspected Offender issued by the Control Yuan. Except when serving on active duty in accordance with law, citizens shall not be tried by military tribunals. Citizens serving on active duty in accordance with law shall not be tried by a Judicial Yuan except for constitutional violations and integrity-related violations of law.

第三十七条 Article 37

中华民族联邦共和国公民应依法执行服兵役义务、依法执行纳税义务、依法执行保护环境义务和依法接受公民义务教育的权利与义务；非经现行法律认可的不可抗力因素的影响，拒不执行与接受前述义务的公民，经司法院审判可剥夺其本国国籍。 Citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall, in accordance with law, perform the duty of military service, the duty to pay taxes, the duty to protect the environment, and the right and duty to receive compulsory citizen education. Citizens who refuse to perform or receive the foregoing duties, except where affected by force majeure factors recognized by current law, may be deprived of the nationality of this State upon adjudication by a Judicial Yuan.

第三十八条 Article 38

《中华民族联邦共和国公民素质教育法》简称《公民素质教育法》，《公民素质教育法》是提升本国公民的人权意识、公民意识、民主意识、法律意识、维权意识、规则意识、责任意识、道德意识、环保意识和纳税意识的素质教育法案。 The Citizen Quality Education Act of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, abbreviated as the Citizen Quality Education Act, shall be a quality education act for improving citizens’ awareness of human rights, citizenship, democracy, law, rights protection, rules, responsibility, morality, environmental protection, and taxation.

第三十九条 Article 39

中华民族联邦共和国公民，因人身安全遭到非法威胁的，因财产安全遭到非法侵犯的，因个人无力保证个人合法人身安全和合法财产安全的，可依法向中华民族联邦共和国任意公民安全局申请公民保护；且公民受到公权机构或公职人员的违法侵犯其权利的，受害公民有权依法向司法院申请国家赔偿。 Where a citizen of the Federal Republic of the China Nation faces an unlawful threat to personal safety, suffers unlawful infringement of property security, or is personally unable to ensure lawful personal safety and lawful property security, the citizen may apply for citizen protection in accordance with law to any Citizen Security Bureau of the Federal Republic of the China Nation. Where a citizen’s rights are unlawfully infringed by a public-power institution or public official, the victimized citizen shall have the right to apply to a Judicial Yuan for state compensation in accordance with law.

第四十条 Article 40

中华民族联邦共和国宪法允许公民通过合法的行为表达诉求，任何公权机构和公职人员不得阻止公民合法的表达诉求，合法的行为包括但不限于游行、结社、示威、静坐等方式，凡作为中华民族联邦共和国的公民，均有义务捍卫自由民主与普世价值。 The Constitution of the Federal Republic of the China Nation permits citizens to express demands through lawful conduct. No public-power institution or public official may prevent citizens from lawfully expressing demands. Lawful conduct includes but is not limited to marches, associations, demonstrations, and sit-ins. Every citizen of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall have the duty to defend freedom, democracy, and universal values.

第五节 宪法的特别条款 Section 5 Special Provisions of the Constitution

第四十一条 Article 41

中华民族联邦共和国禁止一切形式的政治迫害，禁止订立政治迫害类法律，在中华民族联邦共和国的领土上没有政治犯罪的概念、没有政治犯罪的行为和没有政治犯罪的法律。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall prohibit all forms of political persecution and shall prohibit the enactment of laws of political persecution. Within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, there shall be no concept of political crime, no act constituting political crime, and no law on political crime.

第四十二条 Article 42

中华民族联邦共和国应以国家的名义维护海外华人、华侨同胞的人权与利益，在不侵犯他国公民人权的基础上，中华民族联邦共和国军队应以不低于保卫本国领土安全的重视程度维护海外华人、华侨同胞的人权。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall, in the name of the State, protect the human rights and interests of overseas ethnic Chinese and overseas Chinese compatriots. On the basis of not infringing the human rights of citizens of other States, the armed forces of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall protect the human rights of overseas ethnic Chinese and overseas Chinese compatriots with a degree of attention no lower than that given to defending the security of the territory of this State.

第四十三条 Article 43

中华民族联邦共和国的执政党随总统选举而产生，总统所在政党即为执政党，在中华民族联邦共和国注册局注册的政党，均可推选总统候选人参与总统选举。 The ruling party of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be produced through the presidential election. The political party to which the President belongs shall be the ruling party. Any political party registered with the Registration Bureau of the Federal Republic of the China Nation may nominate a presidential candidate to participate in the presidential election.

第一款 中华民族联邦共和国任何注册政党均可推举所属党员参与民选公职人员竞选，任意合法公民均可参与国聘公职人员竞聘，除司法要求外，政党及公民的前述权利不得被剥夺。 Paragraph 1. Any registered political party of the Federal Republic of the China Nation may nominate its party members to participate as candidates in elections for popularly elected public office. Any lawful citizen may compete for employment as a public official employed by a public-power institution. Except as required by the judiciary, the foregoing rights of political parties and citizens shall not be deprived.

第二款 中华民族联邦共和国总统的选举，每党可推出1名总统候选人参与由国家立法院选举委员会组织的总统选举活动；且同一政党不得连续执政超过6届，同一政党连续执政6届的，下一届总统选举该党不得推出总统候选人参与总统选举。 Paragraph 2. In the election of the President of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, each party may nominate one presidential candidate to participate in the presidential election organized by the Election Committee of the National Legislative Yuan. The same political party shall not govern continuously for more than six terms. Where the same political party has governed continuously for six terms, that party may not nominate a presidential candidate to participate in the next presidential election.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国总统每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届，且担任过总统的公民不得再担任副总统、国家立法院院长、国家司法院院长和国家监察院院长；担任过国家立法院院长、国家司法院院长和国家监察院院长的公民，不得再担任总统或副总统。 Paragraph 3. Each term of office of the President of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be five years, and no President may serve more than two terms. A citizen who has served as President may not thereafter serve as Vice President, President of the National Legislative Yuan, President of the National Judicial Yuan, or President of the National Control Yuan. A citizen who has served as President of the National Legislative Yuan, President of the National Judicial Yuan, or President of the National Control Yuan may not thereafter serve as President or Vice President.

第四十四条 Article 44

立法院参议会负责审议立法院法案草案，没有发起法案草案的权力；立法院众议会负责起草发起立法院除教育类法案草案以外的法案草案，没有法案草案终审的权力；立法院参议会和众议会表决程序由常规案表决、重要案表决、特别案表决和常规案二审表决共四种组成。 The Senate of a Legislative Yuan shall be responsible for reviewing draft bills of the Legislative Yuan and shall have no power to initiate draft bills. The House of Representatives of a Legislative Yuan shall be responsible for drafting and initiating draft bills of the Legislative Yuan other than draft education bills, and shall have no power of final review over draft bills. The voting procedures of the Senate and the House of Representatives of a Legislative Yuan shall consist of four types: regular bill vote, major bill vote, special bill vote, and second-review regular bill vote.

第一款 常规案表决需超过80%的现任议员参与表决，并获得60%以上参与表决的议员投赞成票即通过；重要案表决需超过90%的现任议员参与表决，并获得70%以上参与表决的议员投赞成票即通过；常规案二审表决需全体现任议员参与表决，并获得50%以上的赞成票，且反对票少于20%，表决即通过。 Paragraph 1. A regular bill vote shall require the participation of more than 80 percent of all incumbent legislators and shall pass upon affirmative votes by more than 60 percent of the legislators participating in the vote. A major bill vote shall require the participation of more than 90 percent of all incumbent legislators and shall pass upon affirmative votes by more than 70 percent of the legislators participating in the vote. A second-review regular bill vote shall require the participation of all incumbent legislators and shall pass upon more than 50 percent affirmative votes and fewer than 20 percent negative votes.

第二款 立法院特别案表决的通过必须同时满足以下两个条件：一是需全体议员参与投票表决，并获得70%以上的赞成票；二是举行公民投票，其特别案表决为国家立法院的，需占全国70%以上拥有投票权的公民参与投票，并获得70%以上参与投票的公民投赞成票；其特别案表决为省立法院的，需占该省70%以上拥有投票权的公民参与投票，并获得70%以上参与投票的公民投赞成票。 Paragraph 2. Passage of a special bill vote of a Legislative Yuan must simultaneously satisfy the following two conditions: first, all legislators must participate in the vote, and more than 70 percent affirmative votes must be obtained; second, a citizen referendum must be held. Where the special bill vote is of the National Legislative Yuan, more than 70 percent of citizens nationwide possessing voting rights must participate in the vote, and affirmative votes must be cast by more than 70 percent of the citizens participating in the vote. Where the special bill vote is of a provincial Legislative Yuan, more than 70 percent of citizens possessing voting rights in that province must participate in the vote, and affirmative votes must be cast by more than 70 percent of the citizens participating in the vote.

第三款 国家教委会负责起草发起国家立法院教育类法案草案，立法院的常规案表决、重要案表决和常规案二审表决程序适用于国家教委会。 Paragraph 3. The National Education Committee shall be responsible for drafting and initiating draft education bills of the National Legislative Yuan. The procedures for regular bill votes, major bill votes, and second-review regular bill votes of a Legislative Yuan shall apply to the National Education Committee.

第四十五条 Article 45

市立法会的法案草案由该市自治会委员、该市立法会委员、该市辖区任意公民和该市所属镇自治会委员提交，市立法会的表决程序由常规案表决、重要案表决、特别案表决和常规案二审表决共四种组成。 Draft bills of a municipal legislative council shall be submitted by members of the municipal self-governing committee of that municipality, members of the municipal legislative council of that municipality, any citizen within the jurisdiction of that municipality, and members of town self-governing committees belonging to that municipality. The voting procedures of a municipal legislative council shall consist of four types: regular bill vote, major bill vote, special bill vote, and second-review regular bill vote.

第一款 由该市公民提交的法案草案，满足以下两点其一的，市立法会必须接受表决：一是该法案草案获得该市一千名以上公民和五个以上公民团体联署的；二是为提交该法案草案表决而组织的公民集会活动单日内参与人数超过该市总人口数10%的。 Paragraph 1. A municipal legislative council must accept for vote any draft bill submitted by citizens of that municipality if either of the following two conditions is satisfied: first, the draft bill has been jointly signed by more than 1,000 citizens of that municipality and more than five citizen groups; second, the number of participants in a citizen assembly organized for the purpose of submitting the draft bill for vote exceeds 10 percent of the total population of that municipality within a single day.

第二款 市立法会的常规案表决需超过80%的市立法会现任委员参与表决，并获得60%以上参与表决的委员投赞成票即通过；重要案表决需超过90%的市立法会现任委员参与表决，并获得70%以上参与表决的委员投赞成票即通过；常规案二审表决需全体市立法会现任委员参与表决，并获得50%以上的赞成票，且反对票少于20%，表决即通过。 Paragraph 2. A regular bill vote of a municipal legislative council shall require the participation of more than 80 percent of all incumbent members of the municipal legislative council and shall pass upon affirmative votes by more than 60 percent of the members participating in the vote. A major bill vote shall require the participation of more than 90 percent of all incumbent members of the municipal legislative council and shall pass upon affirmative votes by more than 70 percent of the members participating in the vote. A second-review regular bill vote shall require the participation of all incumbent members of the municipal legislative council and shall pass upon more than 50 percent affirmative votes and fewer than 20 percent negative votes.

第三款 市立法会特别案表决的通过必须同时满足以下两个条件：一是需全体市立法会现任委员参与表决，并获得70%以上的赞成票；二是举行公民投票，需占该市70%以上拥有投票权的公民参与投票，并获得70%以上参与投票的公民投赞成票。 Paragraph 3. Passage of a special bill vote of a municipal legislative council must simultaneously satisfy the following two conditions: first, all incumbent members of the municipal legislative council must participate in the vote, and more than 70 percent affirmative votes must be obtained; second, a citizen referendum must be held, in which more than 70 percent of citizens possessing voting rights in that municipality must participate, and affirmative votes must be cast by more than 70 percent of the citizens participating in the vote.

第四十六条 Article 46

在公职人员或军队军人出现廉政违法时，联邦廉政署有权有义务发起廉政违法调查及廉政违法诉讼，联邦廉政署发起廉政违法诉讼经司法院审判罪名成立的，其公职人员或军队军人权责立即失效。 Where a public official or military personnel commits an integrity-related violation of law, the Federal Integrity Agency shall have the right and the duty to initiate an investigation of the integrity-related violation of law and litigation concerning the integrity-related violation of law. Where litigation concerning an integrity-related violation of law initiated by the Federal Integrity Agency is tried by a Judicial Yuan and the charge is established, the powers and responsibilities of such public official or military personnel shall immediately cease to have effect.

第四十七条 Article 47

中华民族联邦共和国禁止设立国家宗教、禁止推崇领袖崇拜主义、禁止从事共产主义活动、禁止从事法西斯主义活动、禁止从事军国主义活动、禁止从事恐怖主义活动、禁止从事反人类活动、禁止从事极端宗教活动，在中华民族联邦共和国的领土上，上述组织、活动与行为均属于违宪违法。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall prohibit the establishment of a state religion, the promotion of leader-worship ideology, engagement in communist activities, engagement in fascist activities, engagement in militarist activities, engagement in terrorist activities, engagement in anti-humanity activities, and engagement in extremist religious activities. Within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, the foregoing organizations, activities, and acts shall all be unconstitutional and unlawful.

第一款 国家允许存在多个合法的宗教，合法宗教的原则在于其教义非排他性和遵守人权自由的基本原则，合法的宗教其教义应以推崇真善美、应以不限制公民的人权自由为基础。 Paragraph 1. The State shall allow the existence of multiple lawful religions. The principles of a lawful religion shall lie in the non-exclusivity of its doctrines and its compliance with the fundamental principles of human rights and freedoms. The doctrines of a lawful religion shall be based on the promotion of truth, goodness, and beauty, and on not restricting citizens’ human rights and freedoms.

第二款 公民的宗教信仰自由，但宗教信仰不能限制公民的人权自由、不能违反现行法律、不得妨碍社会秩序及不得损害公共利益，且公民的人权始终大于宗教及宗教教义。 Paragraph 2. Citizens shall have freedom of religious belief; however, religious belief shall not restrict citizens’ human rights and freedoms, shall not violate current law, shall not obstruct social order, and shall not impair the public interest. Citizens’ human rights shall at all times take precedence over religion and religious doctrine.

第三款 禁止一切形式的政教合一、军政合一、党政合一的组织机构和结社活动，宗教不具有强制约束性，政党不具有国家公权权力；且具有领袖崇拜主义、共产主义、法西斯主义、军国主义、恐怖主义、极端宗教主义和反人类行为的公民，不得任职公职人员，不得加入军队。 Paragraph 3. All forms of organizational institutions and association activities integrating religion and government, military and government, or party and government shall be prohibited. Religion shall have no compulsory binding force, and political parties shall have no state public power. Citizens who engage in leader-worship ideology, communism, fascism, militarism, terrorism, extremist religious ideology, or anti-humanity conduct shall not hold public office and shall not join the armed forces.

第四十八条 Article 48

中华民族联邦共和国的公职人员分为民选公职人员、民聘公职人员和国聘公职人员三种类型，民选公职人员由公民普选产生，民聘公职人员由民选公职人员或民选自治委员提名聘用产生，其他为国聘公职人员。 Public officials of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be divided into three types: popularly elected public officials, public officials appointed by popularly elected public officials or popularly elected self-governing committee members, and public officials employed by public-power institutions. Popularly elected public officials shall be produced by universal election of citizens. Public officials appointed by popularly elected public officials or popularly elected self-governing committee members shall be produced through nomination and appointment by popularly elected public officials or popularly elected self-governing committee members. All other public officials shall be public officials employed by public-power institutions.

第一款 中华民族联邦共和国公职人员中由公民普选产生的有市公民立法委员会委员、市公民教育委员会委员、省参议会参议员、省众议会众议员、护宪大法官和总统府总统；中华民族联邦共和国民选自治委员有镇公民自治委员会委员和市公民自治委员会委员。 Paragraph 1. Among public officials of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, those produced by universal election of citizens shall include members of municipal citizen legislative committees, members of municipal citizen education committees, senators of provincial senates, representatives of provincial houses of representatives, Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices, and the President of the Presidential Office. Popularly elected self-governing committee members of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall include members of town citizen self-governing committees and members of municipal citizen self-governing committees.

第二款 通过镇级行政区所辖公民的投票，可罢免由该镇公民选举产生的镇自治会委员、市自治会委员和市立法会委员的任职；通过市级行政区所辖公民的投票，可罢免由该市公民选举产生的市教委会委员、省参议会参议员和省众议会众议员的任职。 Paragraph 2. Through a vote of the citizens under the jurisdiction of a town-level administrative region, the town self-governing committee members, municipal self-governing committee members, and municipal legislative council members elected by the citizens of that town may be recalled from office. Through a vote of the citizens under the jurisdiction of a municipal administrative region, the municipal education committee members, senators of provincial senates, and representatives of provincial houses of representatives elected by the citizens of that municipality may be recalled from office.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国的所有公职人员必须对外公开个人财产、家庭财产和直系亲属关系，且本人及其法定配偶、未成年子女和生育子女尚未成年的生育配偶必须是拥有中华民族联邦共和国唯一国籍的公民。 Paragraph 3. All public officials of the Federal Republic of the China Nation must publicly disclose their personal property, family property, and lineal-relative relationships. The public official, the public official’s lawful spouse, minor children, and any spouse with whom the public official has biological children who are still minors must be citizens possessing the sole nationality of the Federal Republic of the China Nation.

第四十九条 Article 49

在中华民族联邦共和国境内，记者和律师是维护社会自由和公平的根基所在，除总统府、国防部、各军事管辖区以及国家司法院另行规定的公权机构和区域外，任何公权机构不得阻止记者和律师的工作自由。 Within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, journalists and lawyers are the foundation for safeguarding social freedom and fairness. Except for the Presidential Office, the Department of Defense, military jurisdictions, and public-power institutions and areas separately designated by the National Judicial Yuan, no public-power institution may obstruct the freedom of work of journalists and lawyers.

第五十条 Article 50

在中华民族联邦共和国境内的任何公共场所，除获得音视频设备安装所在市市立法会授权安装的，任何机构和个人不得在公共场所安装音视频设备；且任何机构任何人需要调用公共场所音视频资料的，必须获得音视频设备所在市市监察院和所在省省监察院的双重授权许可。 In any public place within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, no institution or person may install audio or video equipment in a public place unless the installation has been authorized by the municipal legislative council of the municipality where such audio or video equipment is to be installed. Where any institution or person needs to access audio or video data from a public place, dual authorization must be obtained from the municipal Control Yuan of the municipality where the audio or video equipment is located and the provincial Control Yuan of the province where it is located.

第五十一条 Article 51

凡中华民族联邦共和国的法人，未经联邦司法院审判定罪的，任何机构和个人不得对其进行违法限制自由、不得违法要求其提供协助、不得违法没收其财产、不得违法获得其资料。任何行权机构因公职需要法人配合行权行为的，必须获得该法人注册地监察院的依法授权许可，未获得监察院授权许可的，法人有权予以拒绝。 With respect to any legal person of the Federal Republic of the China Nation, no institution or person may unlawfully restrict its freedom, unlawfully require it to provide assistance, unlawfully confiscate its property, or unlawfully obtain its data unless it has been convicted by adjudication of the Federal Judicial Yuan. Where any institution exercising power requires the cooperation of a legal person in the exercise of power for public-office needs, lawful authorization and permission must be obtained from the Control Yuan of the place where the legal person is registered. Without authorization and permission from the Control Yuan, the legal person shall have the right to refuse.

第二章 政府 Chapter II Government

第一节 总统府 Section 1 Presidential Office

第五十二条 Article 52

中华民族联邦共和国设总统府，总统府设总统职位1个，由拥有选举权的公民选举产生，设副总统职位1个，由总统提名；总统府下设国家安全局、国家情报局、国家注册局、国家新闻局和国家人事局。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Presidential Office. The Presidential Office shall have one President position, to be filled by election by citizens possessing the right to vote, and one Vice President position, to be nominated by the President. The Presidential Office shall have under it the National Security Bureau, the National Intelligence Bureau, the National Registration Bureau, the National News Bureau, and the National Personnel Bureau.

第一款 总统提名的副总统，由国家参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，总统可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该提名即驳回，并由总统另选被提名人；总统提名的副总统，经国家参议会三次常规案二审表决仍未通过的，总统可直接提名并任免副总统。 Paragraph 1. The Vice President nominated by the President shall be appointed or removed by the National Senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the President may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the President shall select another nominee. Where the Vice President nominated by the President still does not pass after three second-review regular bill votes by the National Senate, the President may directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Vice President.

第二款 总统和副总统必须是年满35周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在中华民族联邦共和国境内，并在境内生活15年以上的公民。 Paragraph 2. The President and the Vice President must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 35 years of age, and must have been born within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and lived within the territory for more than 15 years.

第三款 中华民族联邦共和国总统在不能履职的情况下的替任顺序为副总统、外交部部长、国防部部长、国安部部长、民生部部长、住建部部长、农业部部长、商贸部部长、财税部部长、能源部部长、交通部部长。 Paragraph 3. Where the President of the Federal Republic of the China Nation is unable to perform duties, the order of succession shall be the Vice President, the Secretary of Foreign Affairs, the Secretary of Defense, the Secretary of Homeland Security, the Secretary of Citizen Livelihood Security, the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development, the Secretary of Agriculture, the Secretary of Commerce and Market Trade, the Secretary of Finance and Taxation, the Secretary of Energy, and the Secretary of Transportation.

第四款 在被替任总统无法恢复其任职的情况下，替任总统的任期为被替任总统任期的剩馀任期，其替任任期不计入选举总统任期，在被替任总统恢复其任职的情况下，替任总统任职资格失效。 Paragraph 4. Where the President whose duties are being assumed is unable to resume office, the term of office of the Acting President shall be the remaining term of the President whose duties are being assumed, and the acting term shall not be counted toward the electoral term limits for President. Where the President whose duties are being assumed resumes office, the office-holding qualification of the Acting President shall cease to have effect.

第五十三条 Article 53

中华民族联邦共和国政府设外交部（外事交流部）、国防部（国家防务部）、国安部（国土安全部）、民生部（公民生活保障部）、住建部（住房与城镇建设部）、农业部（农业与农村发展部）、商贸部（商务与市场贸易部）、财税部（财政与税务部）、能源部（能源与环保发展部）、交通部（交通运输部）共十个直属部级机构。 The Government of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish ten directly subordinate ministerial-level institutions: the Department of Foreign Affairs, the Department of Defense, the Department of Homeland Security, the Department of Citizen Livelihood Security, the Department of Housing and Urban Development, the Department of Agriculture and Rural Development, the Department of Commerce and Market Trade, the Department of Finance and Taxation, the Department of Energy and Environmental Development, and the Department of Transportation.

第五十四条 Article 54

中华民族联邦共和国政府直属部，每部设部长职位1个，由总统提名，设副部长职位3个以下，由部长提名，部长和副部长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 Each directly subordinate department of the Government of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall have one Secretary position, to be nominated by the President, and no more than three Deputy Secretary positions, to be nominated by the Secretary. Each term of office of Secretaries and Deputy Secretaries shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一款 总统提名的部长或部长提名的副部长，由国家参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，提名人可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该提名即驳回，并由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 1. A Secretary nominated by the President or a Deputy Secretary nominated by a Secretary shall be appointed or removed by the National Senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第二款 在国家参议会三次常规案二审表决驳回总统提名的部长或部长提名的副部长后，由国家众议会提名，经国家立法院以重要案表决通过，国家立法院有权独立形成部长或副部长的任免职决议。 Paragraph 2. After the National Senate has rejected three times through second-review regular bill votes a Secretary nominated by the President or a Deputy Secretary nominated by a Secretary, the National House of Representatives shall nominate a candidate, and upon passage by the National Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote, the National Legislative Yuan shall have the power independently to form a resolution on the appointment or removal of the Secretary or Deputy Secretary.

第三款 在国家立法院独立形成部长或副部长的任免职决议时，经三次重要案表决均未通过部长或副部长任免职决议的，由总统直接提名并任免部长，由部长直接提名并任免副部长。 Paragraph 3. Where the National Legislative Yuan independently forms a resolution on the appointment or removal of a Secretary or Deputy Secretary, if the resolution on appointment or removal of the Secretary or Deputy Secretary fails to pass after three major bill votes, the President shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Secretary, and the Secretary shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Deputy Secretary.

第四款 中华民族联邦共和国政府直属部部长和副部长必须是年满30周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在中华民族联邦共和国境内，并在境内生活15年以上的公民。 Paragraph 4. Secretaries and Deputy Secretaries of directly subordinate departments of the Government of the Federal Republic of the China Nation must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 30 years of age, and must have been born within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and lived within the territory for more than 15 years.

第五十五条 Article 55

中华民族联邦共和国总统是国家元首，对外代表中国；总统依宪法统领国家军队，对外签署条约及行使宣战、媾和的权力；总统依法签署法律，签署总统令，行使大赦、特赦的复核权，依法对内发布国家战争动员令和紧急状态令，依法任免文武官员，依法授予国家级荣誉称号及颁发证书。 The President of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the head of State and shall represent China externally. Pursuant to the Constitution, the President shall command the national armed forces, sign treaties externally, and exercise the powers to declare war and make peace. The President shall sign laws in accordance with law, sign presidential orders, exercise the power of review over amnesties and special pardons, issue national war mobilization orders and state-of-emergency orders domestically in accordance with law, appoint and remove civil and military officials in accordance with law, and confer national-level honorary titles and issue certificates in accordance with law.

第二节 省政府 Section 2 Provincial Government

第五十六条 Article 56

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区设省政府，省政府是联邦政府派驻省级行政区的行政权行权机构，省政府隶属于联邦政府；省政府设省长职位1个，由总统提名，设副省长职位2个，由省长提名，省长和副省长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a provincial government. The provincial government shall be the institution exercising administrative power dispatched by the federal government to the provincial administrative region, and shall be subordinate to the federal government. The provincial government shall have one Governor position, to be nominated by the President, and two Deputy Governor positions, to be nominated by the Governor. Each term of office of the Governor and Deputy Governors shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一款 总统提名的省长或省长提名的副省长，由省参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，提名人可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该提名即驳回，并由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 1. A Governor nominated by the President or a Deputy Governor nominated by the Governor shall be appointed or removed by the provincial senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第二款 在省参议会三次常规案二审表决驳回总统提名的省长或省长提名的副省长后，由省众议会提名，经省立法院以重要案表决通过，省立法院有权独立形成省长或副省长的任免职决议。 Paragraph 2. After the provincial senate has rejected three times through second-review regular bill votes a Governor nominated by the President or a Deputy Governor nominated by the Governor, the provincial house of representatives shall nominate a candidate, and upon passage by the provincial Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote, the provincial Legislative Yuan shall have the power independently to form a resolution on the appointment or removal of the Governor or Deputy Governor.

第三款 在省立法院独立形成省长或副省长的任免职决议时，经三次重要案表决均未通过省长或副省长任免职决议的，由总统直接提名并任免省长，由省长直接提名并任免副省长。 Paragraph 3. Where the provincial Legislative Yuan independently forms a resolution on the appointment or removal of a Governor or Deputy Governor, if the resolution on appointment or removal of the Governor or Deputy Governor fails to pass after three major bill votes, the President shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Governor, and the Governor shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Deputy Governors.

第四款 省政府省长和副省长必须是年满30周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在该省辖区内，并在该省辖区内生活10年以上的公民。 Paragraph 4. The Governor and Deputy Governors of a provincial government must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 30 years of age, and must have been born within the jurisdiction of that province and lived within that provincial jurisdiction for more than 10 years.

第五十七条 Article 57

省政府设国防厅（国家防务厅）、国安厅（国土安全厅）、民生厅（公民生活保障厅）、住建厅（住房与城镇建设厅）、农业厅（农业与农村发展厅）、商贸厅（商务与市场贸易厅）、财税厅（财政与税务厅）、能源厅（能源与环保发展厅）、交通厅（交通运输厅）共九个直属厅级机构。 A provincial government shall establish nine directly subordinate department-level institutions: the Department of Defense, the Department of Homeland Security, the Department of Citizen Livelihood Security, the Department of Housing and Urban Development, the Department of Agriculture and Rural Development, the Department of Commerce and Market Trade, the Department of Finance and Taxation, the Department of Energy and Environmental Development, and the Department of Transportation.

第五十八条 Article 58

省政府直属厅，每厅设厅长职位1个，由省长提名，设副厅长职位1个，由厅长提名，厅长和副厅长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 Each directly subordinate department of a provincial government shall have one Director position, to be nominated by the Governor, and one Deputy Director position, to be nominated by the Director. Each term of office of Directors and Deputy Directors shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一款 省长提名的厅长或厅长提名的副厅长，由省参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，提名人可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该提名即驳回，并由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 1. A Director nominated by the Governor or a Deputy Director nominated by the Director shall be appointed or removed by the provincial senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第二款 在省参议会三次常规案二审表决驳回省长提名的厅长或厅长提名的副厅长后，由省众议会提名，经省立法院以重要案表决通过，省立法院有权独立形成厅长或副厅长的任免职决议。 Paragraph 2. After the provincial senate has rejected three times through second-review regular bill votes a Director nominated by the Governor or a Deputy Director nominated by the Director, the provincial house of representatives shall nominate a candidate, and upon passage by the provincial Legislative Yuan through a major bill vote, the provincial Legislative Yuan shall have the power independently to form a resolution on the appointment or removal of the Director or Deputy Director.

第三款 在省立法院独立形成厅长或副厅长的任免职决议时，经三次重要案表决均未通过厅长或副厅长任免职决议的，由省长直接提名并任免厅长，由厅长直接提名并任免副厅长。 Paragraph 3. Where the provincial Legislative Yuan independently forms a resolution on the appointment or removal of a Director or Deputy Director, if the resolution on appointment or removal of the Director or Deputy Director fails to pass after three major bill votes, the Governor shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Director, and the Director shall directly nominate, appoint, and remove the Deputy Director.

第四款 省政府直属厅厅长和副厅长必须是年满25周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在中华民族联邦共和国境内，并在该省辖区内生活10年以上的公民。 Paragraph 4. Directors and Deputy Directors of directly subordinate departments of a provincial government must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 25 years of age, and must have been born within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and lived within the jurisdiction of that province for more than 10 years.

第三节 市政府 Section 3 Municipal Government

第五十九条 Article 59

中华民族联邦共和国市级行政区设市自治政府，简称市政府，市政府是市级行政区的行政权行权机构；市政府与联邦政府之间没有隶属关系，联邦政府在市政府辖区内行使的权力与义务由宪法赋予市政府，再由市政府依法授予联邦政府行权。 A municipal administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a municipal self-governing government, abbreviated as a municipal government. The municipal government shall be the institution exercising administrative power of the municipal administrative region. There shall be no subordinate relationship between a municipal government and the federal government. The powers and duties exercised by the federal government within the jurisdiction of a municipal government shall be vested by the Constitution in the municipal government, and shall then be conferred by the municipal government upon the federal government in accordance with law for exercise.

第六十条 Article 60

市政府设市公民自治委员会，简称市自治会，市自治会委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届；市自治会委员由该市所辖每个镇级行政区的公民从该镇现任镇自治会委员当中选举产生，每个镇级行政区限选举产生1名市自治会委员。 A municipal government shall establish a municipal citizen self-governing committee, abbreviated as a municipal self-governing committee. Each term of office of members of a municipal self-governing committee shall be three years, and no member may serve more than two terms. Members of a municipal self-governing committee shall be elected by citizens of each town-level administrative region under the jurisdiction of the municipality from among the incumbent members of the town self-governing committee of that town. Each town-level administrative region may elect no more than one member of the municipal self-governing committee.

第六十一条 Article 61

市自治会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由该市自治会现任委员互选产生，主席和副主席每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届，且委员任期届满的，不得继续任职主席或副主席。 A municipal self-governing committee shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of that municipal self-governing committee. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairperson shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms. Where a member’s term of office expires, the member may not continue to serve as chairperson or vice-chairperson.

第六十二条 Article 62

市自治会委员不属于在编公职人员，但必须受相关公职人员法律法规要求的约束，且不享受公职人员的薪酬待遇；市自治会委员可兼职除公职人员以外的其他任何合法工作，以及从事除公权机构外包事务以外的任何合法事务。 Members of a municipal self-governing committee shall not be public officials within the authorized establishment, but must be bound by the requirements of relevant laws and regulations governing public officials, and shall not enjoy the salary and benefits of public officials. Members of a municipal self-governing committee may concurrently hold any other lawful work except public office, and may engage in any lawful affairs other than outsourced affairs of public-power institutions.

第一款 市自治会委员有权对该市级行政区辖区内任意一名公职人员发起履职尽责听证会，市自治会委员发起公职人员履职尽责听证会的，被听证公职人员必须参与听证会。 Paragraph 1. A member of a municipal self-governing committee shall have the right to initiate a duty-performance and accountability hearing against any public official within the jurisdiction of that municipal administrative region. Where a member of a municipal self-governing committee initiates a duty-performance and accountability hearing against a public official, the public official subject to the hearing must participate in the hearing.

第二款 市自治会委员发起公职人员履职尽责听证会的，其他现任委员必须参与听证会，且该市辖区内公民均有权要求旁听听证会，除司法要求外，不得拒绝公民的旁听要求。 Paragraph 2. Where a member of a municipal self-governing committee initiates a duty-performance and accountability hearing against a public official, the other incumbent members must participate in the hearing, and citizens within the jurisdiction of that municipality shall have the right to request to observe the hearing. Except as required by the judiciary, citizens’ requests to observe shall not be refused.

第三款 该市辖区内公民有权要求市自治会任意委员组织召开市自治会履职尽责听证会，被要求组织召开听证会的委员及其他委员，非不可抗力的因素，不得拒绝公民的要求。 Paragraph 3. Citizens within the jurisdiction of that municipality shall have the right to request any member of the municipal self-governing committee to organize and convene a duty-performance and accountability hearing of the municipal self-governing committee. The member requested to organize and convene the hearing, as well as the other members, shall not refuse citizens’ request except by reason of force majeure.

第四款 公民要求召开市自治会履职尽责听证会的，要求召开听证会的公民有权有义务邀请任意50名以上本市公民参与听证会；且该市辖区内其他公民均有权要求旁听听证会，除司法要求外，市自治会不得拒绝公民的旁听要求。 Paragraph 4. Where citizens request the convening of a duty-performance and accountability hearing of a municipal self-governing committee, the citizens requesting the hearing shall have the right and the duty to invite 50 or more citizens of that municipality to participate in the hearing. Other citizens within the jurisdiction of that municipality shall have the right to request to observe the hearing, and except as required by the judiciary, the municipal self-governing committee shall not refuse citizens’ requests to observe.

第六十三条 Article 63

市政府设市长职位1个，设副市长职位5个以下，由市自治会提名聘用，市长和副市长每届任期5年，可连任。 A municipal government shall have one Mayor position and no more than five Deputy Mayor positions, to be nominated and employed by the municipal self-governing committee. Each term of office of the Mayor and Deputy Mayors shall be five years, and they may serve consecutive terms.

第一款 市自治会提名聘用的市长或副市长，由市立法会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，市自治会可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该市长或副市长提名即驳回，并由市自治会另选被提名人。 Paragraph 1. A Mayor or Deputy Mayor nominated and employed by the municipal self-governing committee shall be appointed or removed by the municipal legislative council through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the municipal self-governing committee may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination of the Mayor or Deputy Mayor shall be rejected, and the municipal self-governing committee shall select another nominee.

第二款 在市立法会三次常规案二审表决驳回市自治会提名聘用的市长或副市长后，由市立法会提名，经市立法会以重要案表决通过，市立法会有权独立形成市长或副市长的任免职决议。 Paragraph 2. After the municipal legislative council has rejected three times through second-review regular bill votes a Mayor or Deputy Mayor nominated and employed by the municipal self-governing committee, the municipal legislative council shall nominate a candidate, and upon passage by the municipal legislative council through a major bill vote, the municipal legislative council shall have the power independently to form a resolution on the appointment or removal of the Mayor or Deputy Mayor.

第三款 在市立法会独立形成市长或副市长的任免职决议时，经三次重要案表决均未通过市长或副市长任免职决议的，由市自治会直接提名聘用并任免市长或副市长。 Paragraph 3. Where the municipal legislative council independently forms a resolution on the appointment or removal of a Mayor or Deputy Mayor, if the resolution on appointment or removal of the Mayor or Deputy Mayor fails to pass after three major bill votes, the municipal self-governing committee shall directly nominate, employ, appoint, and remove the Mayor or Deputy Mayor.

第六十四条 Article 64

市政府必须设立国防局（国家防务局）、国安局（国土安全局）、公安局（公民安全局）、民生局（公民生活保障局）、财税局（财政与税务局）等直属局级机构，市政府所属其他机构由市政府提案，市立法会决议设立。 A municipal government must establish directly subordinate bureau-level institutions including the Defense Bureau, the Homeland Security Bureau, the Citizen Security Bureau, the Citizen Livelihood Security Bureau, and the Finance and Taxation Bureau. Other institutions under a municipal government shall be established upon proposal by the municipal government and resolution by the municipal legislative council.

第六十五条 Article 65

市政府直属局，每局设局长职位1个，由市长提名聘用，设副局长职位3个以下，由局长提名聘用，局长和副局长每届任期5年，可连任。 Each directly subordinate bureau of a municipal government shall have one Director position, to be nominated and employed by the Mayor, and no more than three Deputy Director positions, to be nominated and employed by the Director. Each term of office of Directors and Deputy Directors shall be five years, and they may serve consecutive terms.

第一款 市长提名的局长或局长提名的副局长，由市立法会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的，提名人可发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的，该提名即驳回，并由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 1. A Director nominated by the Mayor or a Deputy Director nominated by the Director shall be appointed or removed by the municipal legislative council through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator may initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第二款 在市立法会三次常规案二审表决驳回市长提名的局长或局长提名的副局长后，由市立法会提名，经市立法会以重要案表决通过，市立法会有权独立形成局长或副局长的任免职决议。 Paragraph 2. After the municipal legislative council has rejected three times through second-review regular bill votes a Director nominated by the Mayor or a Deputy Director nominated by the Director, the municipal legislative council shall nominate a candidate, and upon passage by the municipal legislative council through a major bill vote, the municipal legislative council shall have the power independently to form a resolution on the appointment or removal of the Director or Deputy Director.

第三款 在市立法会独立形成局长或副局长的任免职决议时，经三次重要案表决均未通过局长或副局长任免职决议的，由市长提名局长，局长提名副局长，经市自治会决议任免。 Paragraph 3. Where the municipal legislative council independently forms a resolution on the appointment or removal of a Director or Deputy Director, if the resolution on appointment or removal of the Director or Deputy Director fails to pass after three major bill votes, the Mayor shall nominate the Director, and the Director shall nominate the Deputy Directors, with appointment or removal to be made by resolution of the municipal self-governing committee.

第四节 镇政府 Section 4 Town Government

第六十六条 Article 66

中华民族联邦共和国镇级行政区设镇自治政府，简称镇政府，镇政府是市政府派驻镇级行政区的自治政府机构，镇政府与联邦政府之间没有隶属关系。 A town-level administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a town self-governing government, abbreviated as a town government. The town government shall be a self-governing government institution dispatched by the municipal government to the town-level administrative region, and there shall be no subordinate relationship between the town government and the federal government.

第六十七条 Article 67

镇政府设镇公民自治委员会，简称镇自治会，镇自治会由11名委员组成，委员由该镇辖区内的公民选举产生；镇自治会委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届，且必须是出生在该镇级行政区辖区内，并在该镇辖区内生活10年以上的公民。 A town government shall establish a town citizen self-governing committee, abbreviated as a town self-governing committee. The town self-governing committee shall consist of 11 members, to be elected by citizens within the jurisdiction of that town. Each term of office of members of a town self-governing committee shall be three years, and no member may serve more than two terms. Members must be citizens who were born within the jurisdiction of that town-level administrative region and have lived within the jurisdiction of that town for more than 10 years.

第六十八条 Article 68

镇自治会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由该镇自治会现任委员互选产生，主席和副主席每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届，且委员任期届满的，不得继续任职主席或副主席。 A town self-governing committee shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of that town self-governing committee. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairperson shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms. Where a member’s term of office expires, the member may not continue to serve as chairperson or vice-chairperson.

第六十九条 Article 69

镇自治会委员不属于在编公职人员，但必须受相关公职人员法律法规要求的约束，且不享受公职人员的薪酬待遇；镇自治会委员可兼职除公职人员以外的其他任何合法工作，以及从事除公权机构外包事务以外的任何合法事务。 Members of a town self-governing committee shall not be public officials within the authorized establishment, but must be bound by the requirements of relevant laws and regulations governing public officials, and shall not enjoy the salary and benefits of public officials. Members of a town self-governing committee may concurrently hold any other lawful work except public office, and may engage in any lawful affairs other than outsourced affairs of public-power institutions.

第一款 镇自治会委员有权对该镇级行政区辖区内任意一名公职人员发起履职尽责听证会，镇自治会委员发起公职人员履职尽责听证会的，被听证公职人员必须参与听证会。 Paragraph 1. A member of a town self-governing committee shall have the right to initiate a duty-performance and accountability hearing against any public official within the jurisdiction of that town-level administrative region. Where a member of a town self-governing committee initiates a duty-performance and accountability hearing against a public official, the public official subject to the hearing must participate in the hearing.

第二款 镇自治会委员发起公职人员履职尽责听证会的，其他现任委员必须参与听证会，且该镇辖区内公民均有权要求旁听听证会，除司法要求外，不得拒绝公民的旁听要求。 Paragraph 2. Where a member of a town self-governing committee initiates a duty-performance and accountability hearing against a public official, the other incumbent members must participate in the hearing, and citizens within the jurisdiction of that town shall have the right to request to observe the hearing. Except as required by the judiciary, citizens’ requests to observe shall not be refused.

第三款 该镇辖区内公民有权要求镇自治会任意委员组织召开镇自治会履职尽责听证会，被要求组织召开听证会的委员及其他委员，非不可抗力的因素，不得拒绝公民的要求。 Paragraph 3. Citizens within the jurisdiction of that town shall have the right to request any member of the town self-governing committee to organize and convene a duty-performance and accountability hearing of the town self-governing committee. The member requested to organize and convene the hearing, as well as the other members, shall not refuse citizens’ request except by reason of force majeure.

第四款 公民要求召开镇自治会履职尽责听证会的，要求召开听证会的公民有权有义务邀请任意30名以上本镇公民参与听证会；且该镇辖区内其他公民均有权要求旁听听证会，除司法要求外，镇自治会不得拒绝公民的旁听要求。 Paragraph 4. Where citizens request the convening of a duty-performance and accountability hearing of a town self-governing committee, the citizens requesting the hearing shall have the right and the duty to invite 30 or more citizens of that town to participate in the hearing. Other citizens within the jurisdiction of that town shall have the right to request to observe the hearing, and except as required by the judiciary, the town self-governing committee shall not refuse citizens’ requests to observe.

第七十条 Article 70

镇政府设镇长职位1个，设副镇长职位2个以下，由镇自治会提名聘用并任免产生，镇长和副镇长每届任期5年，可连任；镇政府下设科级公权机构，每科设科长职位1个，设副科长职位1个，由镇自治会提名聘用并任免产生，科长和副科长每届任期5年，可连任。 A town government shall have one Town Mayor position and no more than two Deputy Town Mayor positions, to be nominated, employed, appointed, and removed by the town self-governing committee. Each term of office of the Town Mayor and Deputy Town Mayors shall be five years, and they may serve consecutive terms. A town government shall have under it section-level public-power institutions. Each section shall have one Section Chief position and one Deputy Section Chief position, to be nominated, employed, appointed, and removed by the town self-governing committee. Each term of office of Section Chiefs and Deputy Section Chiefs shall be five years, and they may serve consecutive terms.

第三章 立法院 Chapter III Legislative Yuan

第一节 国家立法院 Section 1 National Legislative Yuan

第七十一条 Article 71

中华民族联邦共和国设立法院，简称国家立法院，国家立法院设院长职位1个，由国家参议会现任参议员互选产生，每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届；国家立法院设副院长职位1个，由国家众议会现任众议员互选产生，每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Legislative Yuan, abbreviated as the National Legislative Yuan. The National Legislative Yuan shall have one President position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent senators of the National Senate. Each term of office shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms. The National Legislative Yuan shall have one Vice President position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent representatives of the National House of Representatives. Each term of office shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第七十二条 Article 72

国家参议会设参议长职位1个，设副参议长职位1个，由国家参议会现任参议员互选产生，参议长和副参议长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 The National Senate shall have one Speaker position and one Deputy Speaker position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent senators of the National Senate. Each term of office of the Speaker and Deputy Speaker shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第七十三条 Article 73

国家参议会无权起草国家立法院法案草案，参议会有权力有义务审议众议会提交的法案草案和国家教委会提交的教育类法案草案以及公职人员提交的人事任免职书。 The National Senate shall have no power to draft bills of the National Legislative Yuan. The Senate shall have the power and the duty to review draft bills submitted by the House of Representatives, draft education bills submitted by the National Education Committee, and personnel appointment-and-removal documents submitted by public officials.

第一款 国家众议会提交的常规案表决的法案草案，由国家参议会以常规案表决，常规案表决未通过的，众议会可提交对该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 1. A draft bill submitted by the National House of Representatives for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the National Senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the House of Representatives may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第二款 国家众议会提交的重要案表决的法案草案，由国家参议会以重要案表决，重要案表决未通过的即驳回；国家众议会提交的特别案表决的法案草案，由国家参议会以特别案表决，特别案表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 2. A draft bill submitted by the National House of Representatives for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the National Senate through a major bill vote, and if the major bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected. A draft bill submitted by the National House of Representatives for a special bill vote shall be voted on by the National Senate through a special bill vote, and if the special bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第三款 国家教委会提交的常规案表决的教育类法案草案，由国家参议会以常规案表决，常规案表决未通过的，教委会可提交对该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 3. A draft education bill submitted by the National Education Committee for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the National Senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the Education Committee may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第四款 国家教委会提交的重要案表决的教育类法案草案，由国家参议会以重要案表决，重要案表决未通过的即驳回，教育类法案草案不适用于特别案表决程序。 Paragraph 4. A draft education bill submitted by the National Education Committee for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the National Senate through a major bill vote, and if the major bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected. Draft education bills shall not be subject to the special bill vote procedure.

第七十四条 Article 74

国家众议会设众议长职位1个，设副众议长职位1个，由国家众议会现任众议员互选产生，众议长和副众议长每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 The National House of Representatives shall have one Speaker position and one Deputy Speaker position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent representatives of the National House of Representatives. Each term of office of the Speaker and Deputy Speaker shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第七十五条 Article 75

国家众议会负责起草全部特别案表决的国家立法院法案草案，并负责起草除教育类法案草案以外的重要案表决和常规案表决的国家立法院法案草案，众议会无权对法案草案终审。 The National House of Representatives shall be responsible for drafting all National Legislative Yuan draft bills for special bill votes, and shall be responsible for drafting National Legislative Yuan draft bills for major bill votes and regular bill votes other than draft education bills. The House of Representatives shall have no power of final review over draft bills.

第一款 国家众议会众议员提交的常规案表决的法案草案，由国家众议会以常规案表决，常规案表决未通过的，众议员可提交对该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 1. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the National House of Representatives for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the National House of Representatives through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the representative may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第二款 国家众议会众议员提交的重要案表决的法案草案，由国家众议会以重要案表决，重要案表决未通过的即驳回；国家众议会众议员提交的特别案表决的法案草案，由国家众议会以特别案表决，特别案表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 2. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the National House of Representatives for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the National House of Representatives through a major bill vote, and if the major bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the National House of Representatives for a special bill vote shall be voted on by the National House of Representatives through a special bill vote, and if the special bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第七十六条 Article 76

国家立法院议员，由各省级行政区改选，国家立法院院长、副院长、参议长、副参议长、参议员、众议长、副众议长和众议员，任期内不得兼任除国家立法院所属职位以外的其他职位。 Legislators of the National Legislative Yuan shall be elected through re-elections in each provincial administrative region. During their terms of office, the President, Vice President, Speaker of the Senate, Deputy Speaker of the Senate, senators, Speaker of the House of Representatives, Deputy Speaker of the House of Representatives, and representatives of the National Legislative Yuan may not concurrently hold any position other than a position under the National Legislative Yuan.

第二节 省立法院 Section 2 Provincial Legislative Yuan

第七十七条 Article 77

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区设立法院，简称省立法院，省立法院设院长职位1个，由省参议会现任参议员互选产生，每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届；省立法院设副院长职位1个，由省众议会现任众议员互选产生，每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Legislative Yuan, abbreviated as a provincial Legislative Yuan. A provincial Legislative Yuan shall have one President position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent senators of the provincial senate. Each term of office shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms. A provincial Legislative Yuan shall have one Vice President position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent representatives of the provincial house of representatives. Each term of office shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第七十八条 Article 78

省立法院下设参议员议政会，简称省参议会，省参议会设参议长职位1个，设副参议长职位1个，参议长、副参议长和参议员每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届；省参议会参议长和副参议长由省参议会现任参议员互选产生，参议员由该省所辖每个市级行政区的公民从该市现任市自治会委员当中选举产生，每个市级行政区限选举产生1名省参议会参议员。 A provincial Legislative Yuan shall establish a Senate, abbreviated as the provincial senate. The provincial senate shall have one Speaker position and one Deputy Speaker position. Each term of office of the Speaker, Deputy Speaker, and senators shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms. The Speaker and Deputy Speaker of the provincial senate shall be elected by and from among the incumbent senators of the provincial senate. Senators shall be elected by the citizens of each municipal administrative region under the jurisdiction of that province from among the incumbent members of the municipal self-governing committee of that municipality, and each municipal administrative region may elect no more than one senator to the provincial senate.

第七十九条 Article 79

省参议会无权起草省立法院法案草案，省参议会有权力有义务审议省众议会提交的省立法院法案草案以及公职人员提交的人事任免职书。 The provincial senate shall have no power to draft bills of the provincial Legislative Yuan. The provincial senate shall have the power and the duty to review provincial Legislative Yuan draft bills submitted by the provincial house of representatives and personnel appointment-and-removal documents submitted by public officials.

第一款 省众议会提交的常规案表决的法案草案，由省参议会以常规案表决，常规案表决未通过的，众议会可提交对该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 1. A draft bill submitted by the provincial house of representatives for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the house of representatives may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第二款 省众议会提交的重要案表决的法案草案，由省参议会以重要案表决，重要案表决未通过的即驳回；省众议会提交的特别案表决的法案草案，由省参议会以特别案表决，特别案表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 2. A draft bill submitted by the provincial house of representatives for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial senate through a major bill vote, and if the major bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected. A draft bill submitted by the provincial house of representatives for a special bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial senate through a special bill vote, and if the special bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第八十条 Article 80

省立法院下设众议员议政会，简称省众议会，省众议会设众议长职位1个，设副众议长职位1个，众议长、副众议长和众议员每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届；省众议会众议长和副众议长由省众议会现任众议员互选产生，众议员由该省所辖每个市级行政区的公民从该市现任市立法会委员当中选举产生；该市辖区内人口在100万人以下的，限选举产生3名省众议会众议员，该市辖区内人口超过100万人的，后每满100万人增选1名省众议会众议员，最多可增选至7名。 A provincial Legislative Yuan shall establish a House of Representatives, abbreviated as the provincial house of representatives. The provincial house of representatives shall have one Speaker position and one Deputy Speaker position. Each term of office of the Speaker, Deputy Speaker, and representatives shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms. The Speaker and Deputy Speaker of the provincial house of representatives shall be elected by and from among the incumbent representatives of the provincial house of representatives. Representatives shall be elected by the citizens of each municipal administrative region under the jurisdiction of that province from among the incumbent members of the municipal legislative council of that municipality. Where the population within the jurisdiction of that municipality is not more than one million, the municipality shall elect three representatives to the provincial house of representatives; where the population within the jurisdiction of that municipality exceeds one million, one additional representative may be elected for each additional full one million persons thereafter, up to a total of seven representatives.

第八十一条 Article 81

省众议会负责起草全部特别案表决、重要案表决和常规案表决的省立法院法案草案，省众议会无权对省立法院法案草案终审。 The provincial house of representatives shall be responsible for drafting all provincial Legislative Yuan draft bills for special bill votes, major bill votes, and regular bill votes. The provincial house of representatives shall have no power of final review over provincial Legislative Yuan draft bills.

第一款 省众议会众议员提交的常规案表决的法案草案，由省众议会以常规案表决，常规案表决未通过的，众议员可提交对该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 1. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the provincial house of representatives for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial house of representatives through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the representative may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第二款 省众议会众议员提交的重要案表决的法案草案，由省众议会以重要案表决，重要案表决未通过的即驳回；省众议会众议员提交的特别案表决的法案草案，由省众议会以特别案表决，特别案表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 2. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the provincial house of representatives for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial house of representatives through a major bill vote, and if the major bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected. A draft bill submitted by a representative of the provincial house of representatives for a special bill vote shall be voted on by the provincial house of representatives through a special bill vote, and if the special bill vote does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第八十二条 Article 82

省立法院议员，由所属各市级行政区改选，省立法院院长、副院长、参议长、副参议长、参议员、众议长、副众议长和众议员，任期内不得兼任除该省立法院所属职位以外的其他职位。 Legislators of a provincial Legislative Yuan shall be elected through re-elections in the municipal administrative regions to which they belong. During their terms of office, the President, Vice President, Speaker of the Senate, Deputy Speaker of the Senate, senators, Speaker of the House of Representatives, Deputy Speaker of the House of Representatives, and representatives of a provincial Legislative Yuan may not concurrently hold any position other than a position under that provincial Legislative Yuan.

第三节 市立法会 Section 3 Municipal Legislative Council

第八十三条 Article 83

中华民族联邦共和国市级行政区设市公民立法委员会，简称市立法会，市立法会委员由该市所辖每个镇级行政区的公民从该镇现任镇自治会委员当中选举产生，该镇辖区内人口在10万人以下的，限选举产生3名市立法会委员；该镇辖区内人口超过10万人的，后每满10万人增选1名市立法会委员，最多可增选至7名。 A municipal administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a municipal citizen legislative committee, abbreviated as a municipal legislative council. Members of a municipal legislative council shall be elected by the citizens of each town-level administrative region under the jurisdiction of that municipality from among the incumbent members of the town self-governing committee of that town. Where the population within the jurisdiction of that town is not more than 100,000, the town shall elect three members of the municipal legislative council; where the population within the jurisdiction of that town exceeds 100,000, one additional member may be elected for each additional full 100,000 persons thereafter, up to a total of seven members.

第八十四条 Article 84

市立法会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由该市立法会现任委员互选产生；市立法会主席、副主席和委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 A municipal legislative council shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of that municipal legislative council. Each term of office of the chairperson, vice-chairperson, and members of a municipal legislative council shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第八十五条 Article 85

护宪大法官有权对市立法会发起违宪指控听证会，护宪大法官发起市立法会违宪指控听证会的，全体委员必须参与听证会，违宪指控听证会期间，市立法会暂时失去一切职权。 Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall have the power to initiate a hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality against a municipal legislative council. Where Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices initiate such a hearing against a municipal legislative council, all members must participate in the hearing. During the hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality, the municipal legislative council shall temporarily lose all functions and powers.

第一款 市立法会违宪指控听证会后，护宪大法官放弃发起违宪司法审查的，其市立法会的职权恢复；市立法会违宪指控听证会后，护宪大法官发起违宪司法审查的，立即解散该届市立法会，并由公民选举重组。 Paragraph 1. After a hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality against a municipal legislative council, if the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices abandon initiation of constitutional judicial review, the functions and powers of the municipal legislative council shall be restored. After such a hearing, if the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices initiate constitutional judicial review, that municipal legislative council for that term shall be dissolved immediately and reorganized through election by citizens.

第二款 护宪大法官有权对市立法会任意委员发起违宪指控听证会，违宪指控听证会期间，受违宪指控的委员暂时失去委员职权；违宪指控听证会后护宪大法官放弃发起违宪司法审查的，其市立法会委员的职权恢复；违宪指控听证会后护宪大法官发起违宪司法审查的，其市立法会委员的权责立即失效，并由公民重新选举。 Paragraph 2. Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall have the power to initiate a hearing on allegations of unconstitutionality against any member of a municipal legislative council. During the hearing, the member subject to the allegations of unconstitutionality shall temporarily lose the member's functions and powers. If, after the hearing, the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices abandon initiation of constitutional judicial review, the functions and powers of that municipal legislative council member shall be restored. If, after the hearing, the Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices initiate constitutional judicial review, the powers and responsibilities of that municipal legislative council member shall immediately cease, and citizens shall elect a replacement.

第三款 由护宪大法官发起的市立法会违宪司法审查及市立法会委员违宪司法审查由国家司法院负责审查，经国家司法院审判后，市立法会委员违宪罪名成立的，立即永久剥夺其任职公职人员的资格，其违宪罪名不成立的，恢复其自由公民身份。经国家司法院审判后，该市该届市立法会违宪罪名成立的，立即永久剥夺该市该届市立法会所有委员任职公职人员的资格。 Paragraph 3. Constitutional judicial review of a municipal legislative council and constitutional judicial review of a member of a municipal legislative council initiated by Constitution-Guarding Grand Justices shall be reviewed by the National Judicial Yuan. After adjudication by the National Judicial Yuan, where the constitutional violation charge against a member of a municipal legislative council is established, that member shall immediately and permanently be deprived of eligibility to serve as a public official; where the constitutional violation charge is not established, that member's status as a free citizen shall be restored. After adjudication by the National Judicial Yuan, where the constitutional violation charge against the municipal legislative council of that municipality for that term is established, all members of that municipal legislative council for that term shall immediately and permanently be deprived of eligibility to serve as public officials.

第八十六条 Article 86

市立法会委员，由所属各镇级行政区改选，市立法会主席、副主席和委员，任期内不得兼任除该市立法会所属职位以外的其他职位。 Members of a municipal legislative council shall be elected through re-elections in the town-level administrative regions to which they belong. During their terms of office, the chairperson, vice-chairperson, and members of a municipal legislative council may not concurrently hold any position other than a position under that municipal legislative council.

第四章 司法院 Chapter IV Judicial Yuan

第一节 国家司法院 Section 1 National Judicial Yuan

第八十七条 Article 87

中华民族联邦共和国设司法院，简称国家司法院，国家司法院设院长职位1个，设副院长职位1个，由护宪大法官提名，院长和副院长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Judicial Yuan, abbreviated as the National Judicial Yuan. The Federal Investigation Agency shall have no more than 550 Police Inspector rank positions, no more than 1,100 Police Captain rank positions, no more than 5,500 Police Sergeant rank positions, no more than 11,000 Police Corporal rank positions, and no more than 22,000 Police Officer rank positions.

第五节 省监察院 Section 5 Provincial Control Yuan

第一百零九条 Article 109

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区设监察院，简称省监察院，省监察院是国家监察院派驻省级行政区的联邦监察机构；省监察院设院长职位1个，由国家监察院院长提名，设副院长职位1个，由省监察院院长提名，院长和副院长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Control Yuan, abbreviated as a provincial Control Yuan. A provincial Control Yuan shall be a federal control institution dispatched by the National Control Yuan to the provincial administrative region. A provincial Control Yuan shall have one President position, to be nominated by the President of the National Control Yuan, and one Vice President position, to be nominated by the President of the provincial Control Yuan. Each term of office of the President and Vice President shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一百一十条 Article 110

省监察院设首席监察官职位1个，设次席监察官职位3个，设监察官职位5个，由省监察院院长提名；省监察院首席监察官、次席监察官和监察官每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial Control Yuan shall have one Chief Control Officer position, three Deputy Chief Control Officer positions, and five Control Officer positions, to be nominated by the President of the provincial Control Yuan. Each term of office of the Chief Control Officer, Deputy Chief Control Officers, and Control Officers of a provincial Control Yuan shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一百一十一条 Article 111

省监察院院长、副院长、首席监察官、次席监察官和监察官的任免由省参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的由提名人发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回，驳回的由提名人另选被提名人。 The President, Vice President, Chief Control Officer, Deputy Chief Control Officers, and Control Officers of a provincial Control Yuan shall be appointed or removed by the provincial senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator shall initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第六节 市监察院 Section 6 Municipal Control Yuan

第一百一十二条 Article 112

中华民族联邦共和国市级行政区设监察院，简称市监察院，市监察院设院长职位1个，设副院长职位1个，由市自治会提名；市监察院院长和副院长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A municipal administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Control Yuan, abbreviated as a municipal Control Yuan. 第一百一十三条 Article 113

市监察院设首席监察官职位1个，设次席监察官职位1个，设监察官职位3个，由市监察院院长提名；市监察院首席监察官、次席监察官和监察官每届任期5年，任职不得超过4届。 A municipal Control Yuan shall have one Chief Control Officer position, one Deputy Chief Control Officer position, and three Control Officer positions, to be nominated by the President of the municipal Control Yuan. Each term of office of the Chief Control Officer, Deputy Chief Control Officer, and Control Officers of a municipal Control Yuan shall be five years, and no person may serve more than four terms.

第一百一十四条 Article 114

市监察院院长、副院长、首席监察官、次席监察官和监察官的任免由市立法会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的由提名人发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回，驳回的由提名人另选被提名人。 The President, Vice President, Chief Control Officer, Deputy Chief Control Officer, and Control Officers of a municipal Control Yuan shall be appointed or removed by the municipal legislative council through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator shall initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第六章 教委会 Chapter VI Education Committee

第一节 国家教委会 Section 1 National Education Committee

第一百一十五条 Article 115

中华民族联邦共和国设国家公民教育委员会，简称国家教委会，国家教委会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由国家教委会现任委员互选产生；国家教委会主席和副主席每届任期3年，任职不得超过5届。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a National Citizen Education Committee, abbreviated as the National Education Committee. The National Education Committee shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the National Education Committee. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairperson of the National Education Committee shall be three years, and no person may serve more than five terms.

第一百一十六条 Article 116

省级行政区选举产生的国家教委会委员，由该省辖区内市教委会现任委员通过投票，在现任市教委会委员中，分别选举产生1名教师委员和1名任意职业委员。 Members of the National Education Committee elected by a provincial administrative region shall be elected by vote of the incumbent members of the municipal education committees within the jurisdiction of that province from among the incumbent members of those municipal education committees. One teacher member and one member from any occupation shall be elected respectively.

第一百一十七条 Article 117

大学联盟选举产生的国家教委会委员，由各大学联盟选举产生的国家教委会委员候选人互选产生；国家教委会委员候选人，由各大学联盟从国家教委会委员预备候选人中互选产生，每个大学联盟限产生10名国家教委会委员候选人；国家教委会委员预备候选人，由各大学校教委会现任委员互选产生，每个大学限产生3名国家教委会委员预备候选人。 Members of the National Education Committee elected by university alliances shall be elected by and from among the candidates for National Education Committee member elected by each university alliance. Candidates for National Education Committee member shall be elected by each university alliance from among preliminary candidates for National Education Committee member, and each university alliance may produce no more than ten candidates for National Education Committee member. Preliminary candidates for National Education Committee member shall be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the school education committee of each university, and each university may produce no more than three preliminary candidates for National Education Committee member.

第一百一十八条 Article 118

教育应脱离政府的行政干预，除教委会拥有对教育机构的管理权外，各级政府对教育机构没有行政管理权，且政府有义务保障公立教育机构的财政预算。 Education shall be separated from administrative interference by the government. Except for the management power over education institutions held by education committees, governments at all levels shall have no administrative management power over education institutions, and the government shall have the duty to guarantee the fiscal budgets of public education institutions.

第一款 中华民族联邦共和国的大学是高度自治的社会组织，大学教学区不属于所在地行政管理区，大学校教委会仅隶属于国家教委会，大学校教委会有权有义务制订本校教学内容和教学规则。 Paragraph 1. Universities of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be highly autonomous social organizations. University teaching zones shall not belong to the administrative management areas of their locations. A university school education committee shall be subordinate only to the National Education Committee, and shall have the right and the duty to formulate the teaching content and teaching rules of that university.

第二款 国家教委会委员有权有义务起草提交国家立法院所有教育类法案草案，国家教委会委员起草提交的教育类法案草案由国家教委会表决，国家教委会表决通过的，由国家教委会主席提交至国家立法院参议会表决。 Paragraph 2. Members of the National Education Committee shall have the right and the duty to draft and submit all draft education bills of the National Legislative Yuan. Draft education bills drafted and submitted by members of the National Education Committee shall be voted on by the National Education Committee; where passed by vote of the National Education Committee, they shall be submitted by the chairperson of the National Education Committee to the Senate of the National Legislative Yuan for vote.

第三款 国家教委会委员提交的常规案表决的教育类法案草案，由国家教委会以常规案表决，国家教委会常规案表决未通过的，委员可提交该法案草案进行常规案二审表决，国家教委会常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 3. A draft education bill submitted by a member of the National Education Committee for a regular bill vote shall be voted on by the National Education Committee through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote of the National Education Committee does not pass, the member may submit the draft bill for a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote of the National Education Committee does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第四款 国家教委会委员提交的重要案表决的教育类法案草案，由国家教委会以重要案表决，国家教委会重要案表决未通过的即驳回。 Paragraph 4. A draft education bill submitted by a member of the National Education Committee for a major bill vote shall be voted on by the National Education Committee through a major bill vote. If the major bill vote of the National Education Committee does not pass, the draft bill shall be rejected.

第二节 市教委会 Section 2 Municipal Education Committee

第一百一十九条 Article 119

中华民族联邦共和国市级行政区设市公民教育委员会，简称市教委会，市教委会由15名委员组成，委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过5届。 A municipal administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a municipal citizen education committee, abbreviated as a municipal education committee. A municipal education committee shall consist of 15 members. Each term of office of members shall be three years, and no member may serve more than five terms.

第一百二十条 Article 120

市教委会委员由该市拥有选举权的公民从市教委会委员候选人当中投票选举产生，该市辖区内所有中学、小学和初学的校教委会均可推选出1名市教委会委员候选人，且市教委会委员候选人不得少于30人。 Members of a municipal education committee shall be elected by vote of citizens of that municipality who possess voting rights from among candidates for municipal education committee member. The school education committee of every secondary school, primary school, and early learning institution within the jurisdiction of that municipality may recommend one candidate for municipal education committee member, and there shall be no fewer than 30 candidates for municipal education committee member.

第一百二十一条 Article 121

市教委会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由市教委会现任委员互选产生，主席和副主席每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 A municipal education committee shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the municipal education committee. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairperson shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一百二十二条 Article 122

市教委会有权有义务监督辖区内除大学以外的所有教育机构是否执行国家教育法律法案，有权有义务检查辖区内除大学以外的所有学校的教育教学情况，有权有义务执行国家教育法律法案。 A municipal education committee shall have the right and the duty to supervise whether all education institutions within its jurisdiction other than universities are implementing national education laws and acts, shall have the right and the duty to inspect the education and teaching conditions of all schools within its jurisdiction other than universities, and shall have the right and the duty to implement national education laws and acts.

第三节 校教委会 Section 3 School Education Committee

第一百二十三条 Article 123

中华民族联邦共和国所有大学、中学、小学和初学必须设立学校公民教育委员会，简称校教委会，校教委会为学校的最高行政管理机构；大学校教委会由15名委员组成，中学、小学和初学校教委会由11名委员组成。 All universities, secondary schools, primary schools, and early learning institutions of the Federal Republic of the China Nation must establish a school citizen education committee, abbreviated as a school education committee. A school education committee shall be the highest administrative management institution of the school. A university school education committee shall consist of 15 members, and the school education committee of a secondary school, primary school, or early learning institution shall consist of 11 members.

第一百二十四条 Article 124

大学校教委会委员中，任教于该大学的教授委员为11人，就读于该大学的学生委员为3人，独立委员为1人；中学、小学和初学校教委会委员中，任教于该校的教师委员为7人，就读于该校的学生的监护人委员为3人，独立委员为1人。 Among the members of a university school education committee, there shall be 11 professor members teaching at that university, three student members enrolled at that university, and one independent member. Among the members of the school education committee of a secondary school, primary school, or early learning institution, there shall be seven teacher members teaching at that school, three student-guardian members who are guardians of students enrolled at that school, and one independent member.

第一百二十五条 Article 125

大学校教委会教授委员的选举，由该校教授委员候选人互选产生；学生委员的选举，由该校学生委员候选人互选产生；独立委员的选举，由该校在校学生选举产生。中学、小学和初学校教委会教师委员的选举，由该校教师委员候选人互选产生；学生监护人委员的选举，由该校学生监护人委员候选人互选产生；独立委员的选举，由该校在校学生的监护人选举产生。 Professor members of a university school education committee shall be elected by and from among the professor-member candidates of that university. Student members shall be elected by and from among the student-member candidates of that university. The independent member shall be elected by the enrolled students of that university. Teacher members of the school education committee of a secondary school, primary school, or early learning institution shall be elected by and from among the teacher-member candidates of that school. Student-guardian members shall be elected by and from among the student-guardian-member candidates of that school. The independent member shall be elected by the guardians of the enrolled students of that school.

第一百二十六条 Article 126

大学校教委会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由校教委会现任委员互选产生；校教委会主席、副主席、教授委员和独立委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过5届；校教委会学生委员每届任期1年，任职不得超过3届。 A university school education committee shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the school education committee. Each term of office of the chairperson, vice-chairperson, professor members, and independent member of the school education committee shall be three years, and no person may serve more than five terms. Each term of office of student members of the school education committee shall be one year, and no student member may serve more than three terms.

第一百二十七条 Article 127

大学校教委会委员候选人中，教授委员候选人为该校在岗全职教授，学生委员候选人为该校全日制在校学生，独立委员候选人为合法公民；该校的在职教授和在校学生，不得成为该校校教委会的独立委员。 Among the candidates for members of a university school education committee, professor-member candidates shall be full-time professors in post at that university, student-member candidates shall be full-time enrolled students of that university, and independent-member candidates shall be lawful citizens. Professors in service and enrolled students of that university may not become independent members of the school education committee of that university.

第一百二十八条 Article 128

中学、小学和初学校教委会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位1个，由该校校教委会现任委员互选产生；校教委会主席、副主席、教师委员和独立委员每届任期3年，任职不得超过5届；校教委会学生监护人委员每届任期1年，任职不得超过2届。 The school education committee of a secondary school, primary school, or early learning institution shall have one chairperson position and one vice-chairperson position, to be elected by and from among the incumbent members of that school's school education committee. Each term of office of the chairperson, vice-chairperson, teacher members, and independent member of the school education committee shall be three years, and no person may serve more than five terms. Each term of office of student-guardian members of the school education committee shall be one year, and no student-guardian member may serve more than two terms.

第一百二十九条 Article 129

中学、小学和初学校教委会委员候选人中，教师委员候选人为该校在岗全职教师，学生监护人委员候选人为该校在校学生的监护人，独立委员候选人为合法公民；该校的在职教师和在校学生的监护人，不得成为该校校教委会的独立委员。 Among the candidates for members of the school education committee of a secondary school, primary school, or early learning institution, teacher-member candidates shall be full-time teachers in post at that school, student-guardian-member candidates shall be guardians of enrolled students of that school, and independent-member candidates shall be lawful citizens. Teachers in service at that school and guardians of enrolled students of that school may not become independent members of that school's school education committee.

第七章 储委会 Chapter VII Reserve Committee

第一节 储委会联合会议 Section 1 Joint Meeting of the Reserve Committee

第一百三十条 Article 130

中华民族联邦共和国公民储备委员会联合会议为储委会的最高决策机构，公民储备委员会联合会议由储委会主席或副主席召集主持，公民储备委员会委员出席，省储备委员会副主席和省储备银行副行长有权列席。 The Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be the highest decision-making body of the Reserve Committee. The Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee shall be convened and presided over by the chairperson or a vice-chairperson of the Reserve Committee. Members of the Citizen Reserve Committee shall attend, and vice-chairpersons of provincial reserve committees and deputy governors of provincial reserve banks shall have the right to attend as non-voting participants.

第一百三十一条 Article 131

出席公民储备委员会联合会议的国家储备委员会主席和副主席、国家储备委员会委员、省储备委员会主席和省储备银行行长拥有相等的表决权，列席公民储备委员会联合会议的省储备委员会副主席和省储备银行副行长拥有知情权、监督权和异议权。 The chairperson and vice-chairpersons of the National Reserve Committee, members of the National Reserve Committee, chairpersons of provincial reserve committees, and governors of provincial reserve banks who attend the Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee shall have equal voting rights. Vice-chairpersons of provincial reserve committees and deputy governors of provincial reserve banks who attend the Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee as non-voting participants shall have the right to know, the right of supervision, and the right of objection.

第一百三十二条 Article 132

中华民族联邦共和国法定货币的新增发行，由公民储备委员会联合会议决议发行，经联合会议全票通过的可直接发行，非全票通过的，由全民公投决定，超过50%的可投票公民投票同意方可发行，反之则否决发行。 Additional issuance of the legal tender of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be resolved for issuance by the Joint Meeting of the Citizen Reserve Committee. Where the Joint Meeting passes it unanimously, it may be issued directly. Where it is not passed unanimously, issuance shall be decided by a nationwide referendum, and may proceed only if more than 50 percent of citizens eligible to vote cast affirmative votes; otherwise, issuance shall be rejected.

第二节 国家储备委员会 Section 2 National Reserve Committee

第一百三十三条 Article 133

中华民族联邦共和国设国家储备委员会，国家储备委员会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位2个，主席和副主席每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 The Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a National Reserve Committee. The National Reserve Committee shall have one chairperson position and two vice-chairperson positions. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairpersons shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一款 国家储备委员会主席和副主席由国家储备委员会现任委员互选产生，主席或副主席任期结束的可继续任职委员，但委员任期结束的不得再继续任职主席或副主席，且委员每年更换不得超过5人。 Paragraph 1. The chairperson and vice-chairpersons of the National Reserve Committee shall be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the National Reserve Committee. A chairperson or vice-chairperson whose term of office ends may continue to serve as a member, but a member whose term of office ends may not continue to serve as chairperson or vice-chairperson. No more than five members may be replaced each year.

第二款 总统提名的国家储备委员会委员，由国家参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的由提名人发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回，驳回的由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 2. Members of the National Reserve Committee nominated by the President shall be appointed or removed by the National Senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator shall initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第三款 国家储备委员会主席、副主席和委员必须是年满35周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在中华民族联邦共和国境内，并在境内生活10年以上的公民。 Paragraph 3. The chairperson, vice-chairpersons, and members of the National Reserve Committee must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 35 years of age, and must have been born within the territory of the Federal Republic of the China Nation and lived within the territory for more than 10 years.

第一百三十四条 Article 134

中华民族联邦共和国国家储备委员会负责国家金融制度的制订、经济政策的制定，且国家储备委员会有义务配合总统及国家联邦政府制定国家金融政策法规。 The National Reserve Committee of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be responsible for formulating the national financial system and economic policy, and the National Reserve Committee shall have the duty to cooperate with the President and the national federal government in formulating national financial policies and regulations.

第三节 省储备委员会 Section 3 Provincial Reserve Committee

第一百三十五条 Article 135

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区设省储备委员会，省储备委员会设主席职位1个，设副主席职位2个，主席和副主席每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a provincial reserve committee. A provincial reserve committee shall have one chairperson position and two vice-chairperson positions. Each term of office of the chairperson and vice-chairpersons shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一款 省储备委员会主席和副主席由省储备委员会现任委员互选产生，主席或副主席任期结束的可继续任职委员，但委员任期结束的不得再继续任职主席或副主席，且委员每年更换不得超过3人。 Paragraph 1. The chairperson and vice-chairpersons of a provincial reserve committee shall be elected by and from among the incumbent members of the provincial reserve committee. A chairperson or vice-chairperson whose term of office ends may continue to serve as a member, but a member whose term of office ends may not continue to serve as chairperson or vice-chairperson. No more than three members may be replaced each year.

第二款 省长提名的省储备委员会委员，由省参议会以常规案表决任免产生，常规案表决未通过的由提名人发起常规案二审表决，常规案二审表决未通过的即驳回，驳回的由提名人另选被提名人。 Paragraph 2. Members of a provincial reserve committee nominated by the Governor shall be appointed or removed by the provincial senate through a regular bill vote. If the regular bill vote does not pass, the nominator shall initiate a second-review regular bill vote. If the second-review regular bill vote does not pass, the nomination shall be rejected, and the nominator shall select another nominee.

第三款 省储备委员会主席、副主席和委员必须是年满30周岁的中华民族联邦共和国公民，且必须是出生在该省级行政区辖区内，并在该省辖区内生活10年以上的公民。 Paragraph 3. The chairperson, vice-chairpersons, and members of a provincial reserve committee must be citizens of the Federal Republic of the China Nation who have attained 30 years of age, and must have been born within the jurisdiction of that provincial administrative region and lived within the jurisdiction of that province for more than 10 years.

第一百三十六条 Article 136

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区省储备委员会负责该省金融制度的制订、经济政策的制定，且省储备委员会有义务配合省长及省政府制定该省的金融政策法规。 The provincial reserve committee of a provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall be responsible for formulating the financial system and economic policy of that province, and the provincial reserve committee shall have the duty to cooperate with the Governor and the provincial government in formulating the financial policies and regulations of that province.

第四节 公民储备银行 Section 4 Citizen Reserve Bank

第一百三十七条 Article 137

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区设公民储备银行，简称省储行，公民储备银行设行长职位1个，副行长职位2个，由公民储备银行董事会决议任免，行长和副行长每届任期5年，任职不得超过2届。 A provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall establish a Citizen Reserve Bank, abbreviated as a provincial reserve bank. A Citizen Reserve Bank shall have one Governor position and two Deputy Governor positions, to be appointed or removed by resolution of the board of directors of the Citizen Reserve Bank. Each term of office of the Governor and Deputy Governors shall be five years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一百三十八条 Article 138

公民储备银行设董事会，由9名董事组成，设董事长职位1个，副董事长职位2个，由全体董事互选产生，公民储备银行董事会董事长、副董事长和董事每届任期3年，任职不得超过2届。 A Citizen Reserve Bank shall establish a board of directors consisting of nine directors. The board shall have one chairperson position and two vice-chairperson positions, to be elected by and from among all directors. Each term of office of the chairperson, vice-chairpersons, and directors of the board of directors of a Citizen Reserve Bank shall be three years, and no person may serve more than two terms.

第一百三十九条 Article 139

公民储备银行董事会董事的产生，由本公民储备银行股东大会决议产生4名董事，由本公民储备银行所辖成员银行选派3名董事，由本省省储备委员会选派1名董事，由本省财政与税务厅选派1名董事。 The directors of the board of directors of a Citizen Reserve Bank shall be produced as follows: four directors shall be produced by resolution of the shareholders' meeting of that Citizen Reserve Bank; three directors shall be selected and appointed by the member banks under the jurisdiction of that Citizen Reserve Bank; one director shall be selected and appointed by the provincial reserve committee of that province; and one director shall be selected and appointed by the Department of Finance and Taxation of that province.

第一百四十条 Article 140

中华民族联邦共和国省级行政区公民储备银行代表该省公开市场，参与金融制度的制订、经济政策的制定以及货币的流通，公民储备银行不属于公权机构，其雇员不属于公职人员，但必须受相关公职人员法律法规要求的约束。 The Citizen Reserve Bank of a provincial administrative region of the Federal Republic of the China Nation shall represent the open market of that province and participate in the formulation of the financial system, the formulation of economic policy, and the circulation of currency. A Citizen Reserve Bank shall not be a public-power institution, and its employees shall not be public officials, but they must be bound by the requirements of relevant laws and regulations governing public officials.
